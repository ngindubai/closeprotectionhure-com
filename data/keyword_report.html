Core Finding

The security/bodyguard niche has an unusual profile: very low search volume but very high conversion value and very low competition. A single conversion from "bodyguard hire Lagos" is worth $2,000-5,000/day. The entire first page of Google for most city-specific security queries is either empty, filled with generic directory listings, or dominated by local-language sites with no English competition.

The informational funnel ("is Lagos safe", "is Dubai safe") generates 10-100x the volume of commercial queries and captures the same audience earlier in their journey. Risk assessment pages are the traffic engine; city service pages are the conversion engine.

Language Advantage

10 of 15 P1 cities operate in non-English languages (Spanish, Portuguese, Turkish, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Thai, Urdu, Indonesian). English-language security content for these cities has virtually zero competition. Corporate security directors and HNWI PAs search in English regardless of the destination city's language.

Regional Escalation Opportunity

April 2026 Middle East escalation has caused a surge in security-related searches for Dubai, Riyadh, Doha, Kuwait. "Is Dubai safe now", "Dubai missile threat", and "executive protection Dubai" are seeing elevated demand. This is a time-sensitive opportunity to capture newly created search demand.

Quick Win Analysis

#1 Security Drivers

Lowest competition across ALL 15 cities. Carjacking and kidnapping fears drive search behaviour. Almost zero quality pages exist for "security driver {city}" in English. Every page we publish will likely rank page 1 within weeks.

Best cities: Johannesburg, Lagos, Mexico City, Bogota, Sao Paulo

#2 Bodyguard Hire

Direct buyer intent. The person typing "bodyguard hire Lagos" is ready to purchase. English content gaps are massive in non-English-speaking cities. Only Johannesburg and Dubai have meaningful competition.

Best cities: Riyadh, Bogota, Karachi, Istanbul, Moscow

#3 Risk Assessments (Info)

"Is Dubai safe" gets 10-30K searches/month. "Is Bangkok safe" is similar. These pages are 10-100x higher traffic than any commercial query. They capture the buyer at the start of their journey and funnel them to security services.

Best cities: Dubai, Bangkok, Istanbul, Mexico City, Johannesburg

Key Findings

Dubai Dominates All Service Autocomplete

Dubai appears as the top city in autocomplete for close protection, private security, event security, and executive protection queries. This confirms Dubai as the highest-demand P1 city, but also the most competitive. Content for Dubai must be premium quality to rank.

Named competitors in Dubai autocomplete: Transguard, Amana, MAB, Sobha

War Queries Dominating Dubai + Riyadh

"Is Dubai safe from war", "is Dubai safe from Iran", "is Dubai safe now from Iran", "is Riyadh safe from war" all appear in autocomplete. This is a time-sensitive, unprecedented content opportunity. Iran escalation has created entirely new search demand that did not exist 6 months ago.

Women's Safety: 6 Cities

Mumbai, Istanbul, Bangkok, Nairobi, Dubai, and Jakarta all show "safe for women" / "solo female travellers" as a dominant autocomplete pattern. This signals a dedicated content angle: female executive travel security pages on all 6 city risk assessments. Possible dedicated content hub.

Zero Brand Competition Outside Dubai

No branded security company appears in Google Autocomplete for any P1 city bodyguard/EP query except Dubai (Transguard, Amana, MAB). Lagos shows Halogen and Maximus for generic "security company" only. The space is wide open for brand capture.
