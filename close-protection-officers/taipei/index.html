Venue Advance Work

The CPO or an advance officer conducts physical venue reconnaissance at hotels, corporate offices, and meeting venues across Xinyi District corporate and luxury retail hub, Da'an residential, and Zhongzheng government district before the principal's arrival. Operations Controller Support

Every CPO deployment in Taipei is supported by a remote operations controller maintaining timed-check communication with the deployed officer, tracking vehicle movements, and holding contingency protocols for security incident, medical emergency, and principal extraction. The controller is the coordination hub between the CPO, the security driver, and any venue security management.

Transport Integration
