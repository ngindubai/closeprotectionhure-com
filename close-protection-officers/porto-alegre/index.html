Policia Federal Licensing Framework

Private security companies in Brazil, including CPO operators, must hold Policia Federal licences under Portaria 387/2006 (Ministerio da Justica). Individual CPOs carry Policia Federal certifications for close protection functions under the applicable Portaria 387 module. All CPOs engaged for Porto Alegre assignments hold current Policia Federal licences. SESP-RS 2024 records Porto Alegre as high-crime, making the Policia Federal licensing standard a practical operational requirement.

Kidnap and Express Crime Counter-Protocols

SESP-RS 2024 records express kidnapping targeting high-income individuals in Porto Alegre's commercial and residential areas. CPO operational planning in Porto Alegre addresses the express kidnapping risk vectors: route selection avoiding documented approaches, departure timing management from predictable locations (hotels, corporate offices, event venues), vehicle security disciplines, principal communication check-in procedures, and the emergency contact chain for crisis management resources.

Moinhos de Vento and Bela Vista Operations

CPO operational work in Porto Alegre is concentrated in Moinhos de Vento (primary HNWI and senior corporate district), Bela Vista, Auxiliadora, and the Centro Historico business district. Pre-advance work covers principal accommodation, primary meeting venues, restaurants, and private clubs. Moinhos de Vento's established infrastructure provides a better operational baseline than peripheral areas; the CPO team's advance work calibrates the level of intervention to the specific area of each engagement.

Armed and Unarmed Detail Options

Policia Federal-licensed armed CPOs with registered SINARM (Sistema Nacional de Armas) firearms are available for principals with elevated threat profiles in Porto Alegre. For most senior corporate visitors conducting business in Moinhos de Vento and Bela Vista, unarmed Policia Federal-licensed CPOs with express kidnapping counter-protocols and vehicle security disciplines provide an appropriate baseline. The threat assessment for the specific principal determines the appropriate option.

Post-Flood Operational Awareness

Salgado Filho Airport Arrival Protocol

The CPO team meets the principal inside Salgado Filho International Airport (POA, 8km from city centre) arrivals hall, manages the terminal exit, and conducts the vehicle security check before transfer to the Moinhos de Vento hotel cluster. SESP-RS 2024 records robbery targeting arriving passengers at POA and on airport approach routes. The arrivals hall collection protocol eliminates the kerb zone exposure.
