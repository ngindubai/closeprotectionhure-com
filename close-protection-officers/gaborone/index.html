Operational detail for Gaborone

Private Security Industry Regulation Act Licensing

Private security operations in Botswana are governed by the Private Security Industry Regulation Act. CPO companies must hold a current operating licence issued under this Act, and individual operators require registration with the relevant national authority. Our Gaborone CPO partners provide current licensing documentation to clients before deployment. Operators licensed under South Africa's PSIRA framework cannot carry that authorisation into Botswana; a Botswana Private Security Industry Regulation Act licence is mandatory for every in-country CPO deployment.

GBE Airport Transfer: 15km Corridor

Sir Seretse Khama International Airport (GBE) is approximately 15 kilometres north of central Gaborone. The transfer to the CBD, Fairgrounds, and principal hotel zone is straightforward and generally completes in 15 to 25 minutes. The crime risk on the GBE corridor is low by regional standards, but the standard CPO airport protocol applies: inside-terminal collection before the principal exits to the vehicle bay, confirmed vehicle positioning, and a brief route assessment for current conditions.

Diamond Sector and Corporate Team Configuration

Gaborone hosts Debswana (the De Beers-Botswana government joint venture) and serves as the regional hub for diamond trading and rough diamond sorting following Botswana's agreements with De Beers. Diamond industry executives, rough diamond valuation personnel, and international buyers form a distinct CPO client category. The appropriate configuration is typically a discreet 2-person team with knowledge of the Gaborone International Financial Centre, the Diamond Technology Park, and the Fairgrounds business district. Diplomatic visitors to the SADC headquarters also represent a regular assignment type.

Gaborone Movement Corridors

Gaborone is a low-density planned capital with straightforward movement corridors. The CBD, Fairgrounds, and Broadhurst industrial areas are the primary business zones; the Phakalane and Molapo areas provide residential and hotel options. CPO movement is unrestricted across the main districts under normal conditions. After-dark movement requires standard precautions around the bus rank and outer commercial areas. The Botswana Police Service (emergency: 999) provides reliable response within the main urban areas.

South Africa Border Proximity and Cross-Border Context

Gaborone is located approximately 15 kilometres from the South Africa border at Tlokweng Gate, creating a specific cross-border logistics dimension for CPO assignments. Medical evacuation to Johannesburg and business travel to South Africa via road or air are routine. CPO coverage for Johannesburg or South Africa-based elements of an assignment requires a separate South Africa operator engagement; Botswana licensing does not extend across the border. The proximity to South Africa is a planning asset for medevac logistics rather than a security risk factor.

Medical Evacuation: Johannesburg and Local Options

Gaborone has reasonable private medical facilities by regional standards, including Bokamoso Private Hospital. For serious trauma, complex surgery, or specialist treatment, medical evacuation to Johannesburg is the standard response: Gaborone is approximately 1 hour by air or 3 to 4 hours by road from Johannesburg's major hospitals. CPO deployment planning confirms the client's medevac insurance covers Botswana and identifies both the Bokamoso Hospital pathway for in-country care and the Johannesburg air evacuation option before each assignment.
