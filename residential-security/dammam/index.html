What this covers

Operational detail for Dammam

Property Security Survey

A survey of your Dammam, Dhahran, or Half Moon Bay residence, covering perimeter integrity, access control points, CCTV coverage, lighting, and any structural vulnerabilities. Residential properties in Dammam range from standalone villas in the Dhahran and Half Moon Bay residential areas to apartment compounds in central Dammam and purpose-built company compounds for oil sector assignees. The survey methodology adapts to property type and the specific risk profile of the neighbourhood and sector.

Neighbourhood Threat Assessment

Dammam and the Eastern Province present a moderate residential risk profile weighted toward oil sector-specific threats. The FCDO Saudi Arabia travel advice (2024) records a terrorism risk against oil infrastructure and Western interests. The Eastern Province has historically been a focus of this threat given its concentration of oil production assets. Criminal activity targeting private residences is at lower levels than in comparable non-Gulf cities; the primary residential security concern is targeted threat related to the principal's sector and nationality rather than opportunistic burglary.

Access Control and Perimeter

Access control for Dammam and Dhahran residences covers compound vehicle gate procedures, visitor verification, contractor and domestic staff management, and perimeter monitoring. Oil sector company compounds typically have their own security infrastructure managed by the employer; residential security for individual assignees complements and supplements the company compound protocols rather than replacing them.

Domestic Staff Vetting

Domestic staff vetting in Saudi Arabia is conducted through the employer sponsorship system (kafala), which requires domestic workers to hold valid employment visas and be registered with the General Directorate of Passports. Vetting covers status verification, identity confirmation, and reference checks from previous Saudi employers. All household employees must hold valid Saudi residency documentation; status verification is the first step of any domestic staff vetting engagement in the Kingdom.

Emergency Response Protocols

Emergency baseline for Dammam residences: 999 for police, 911 for general emergency, 920000999 for ambulance. The British Embassy Riyadh (Eastern Province line) at +966 11 488 0077 and the US Consulate General Dhahran at +966 13 330 3200 are held in the household emergency plan. The US Consulate General in Dhahran is specifically responsible for the Eastern Province and is the primary US consular contact for residents in Dammam and the oil sector area.

Technology and Monitoring

CCTV installation, alarm integration, and remote monitoring through Saudi-licensed technology partners. Saudi Arabia has specific regulations on private security technology installations through the Council of Ministers Resolution No. 58 framework. CCTV coverage of public or shared external areas must comply with the applicable Saudi regulations. Remote monitoring with defined escalation procedures and Saudi emergency service coordination is standard for all Dammam residential deployments.
