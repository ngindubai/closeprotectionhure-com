Kampala Event Landscape

Kampala is Uganda's capital and the primary venue for East African business conferences, development sector gatherings, bilateral government meetings, and regional investment forums. The Nakasero and Kololo districts house the principal international hotel properties and government offices, with the Serena Hotel Kampala, Sheraton Kampala, and Pearl of Africa Hotel providing the primary conference infrastructure. Kampala's growing economic profile as a sub-Saharan Africa investment destination has generated an expanding international event calendar.

Uganda Police Force Licensing

Private security in Uganda is governed by the Private Security Organisations Act, with the Uganda Police Force (UPF) maintaining the Private Security registry of approved operators. Security companies must hold UPF-endorsed licences and individual operatives require UPF registration. Armed security is available through licensed operators. Foreign security personnel require work permits and must operate through Ugandan-licensed firms. Our Kampala event security partners hold current UPF licensing. Documentation is provided to event organisers before deployment.

ADF Terrorism Awareness

The Allied Democratic Forces (ADF), an IS-affiliated group operating primarily from eastern DRC, has conducted attacks in Uganda including the 2021 Kampala bombings in which three explosions targeted an intersection near parliament and a police station. The FCDO rates the terrorism risk in Uganda as high. Terrorism-aware event security in Kampala includes vehicle access control at event entrances, x-ray and wand screening for all attendees, and venue perimeter management proportionate to the event's international profile.

Traffic and Boda Boda Management

Kampala traffic is consistently congested and chaotic. Boda boda motorcycle taxis create a specific threat: snatching from vehicles in traffic is well documented. Event delegate transport uses windows-up management in congested areas and PSIRA-licensed operators briefed on the specific Kampala boda boda risk environment. Event programmes build generous transit time into all delegate movement between venues. Night driving is strongly discouraged and managed accordingly.

Entebbe International Airport Delegate Transfers

Entebbe International Airport (EBB) is located approximately 40 kilometres south-west of Kampala on the shores of Lake Victoria. The transfer to Kampala's Nakasero and Kololo hotel zone takes 45 to 75 minutes depending on traffic on the Entebbe-Kampala highway. Our event security team coordinates all delegate arrivals and departures with UPF-licensed transport. The Entebbe highway approach is the single main road: traffic on this corridor is the primary delegate transfer variable.

Medical Provision and Emergency Planning

Medical facilities in Kampala are limited by international standards. International SOS and AGA Khan Hospital Kampala provide the best available private medical care. For complex cases requiring specialist treatment, evacuation to Nairobi or South Africa is the standard option. All Kampala event security assessments include a medical provision plan with the nearest capable facility identified and the medical evacuation route documented.
