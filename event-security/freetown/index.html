Freetown Event Landscape

Freetown's international event calendar is dominated by two primary sectors. Mining and natural resources events -- connected to Sierra Leone's diamond sector (SL Mining, Koidu operations), rutile mining (Sierra Leone is among the world's leading rutile producers), bauxite, iron ore, and the large artisanal gemstone sector -- generate licensing negotiations, investor meetings, and environmental compliance events in Freetown as the regulatory capital. The very large international NGO and humanitarian sector maintains significant Sierra Leone country programmes (UNHCR, WFP, UNICEF, and many implementing partners), generating inter-agency coordination meetings, donor events, and cluster system gatherings throughout the year. The Radisson Blu Mammy Yoko Hotel and the Country Lodge are the principal conference venues for international events in the Aberdeen peninsula area.

Private Security Industry Act Licensing

Event security providers in Freetown must hold current licensing under the Private Security Industry Act (Sierra Leone). Individual guards require documented registration. Verify operator licensing and guard registration before engagement. The FCDO advises a high degree of caution throughout Sierra Leone; the high-risk environment means event security must address the documented armed robbery and post-election unrest risks alongside standard access management. An operator with current Sierra Leone licensing and Freetown operational experience in the post-conflict environment is required. Source: Private Security Industry Act, Sierra Leone; FCDO Sierra Leone travel advice (2026).

Lungi Airport Delegate Transfer Management

Lungi International Airport (FNA) sits across the Sierra Leone River estuary from Freetown, creating a unique logistical security challenge for event delegate management. Helicopter transfer (approximately 10 minutes from Lungi to Freetown's Aberdeen waterfront landing point) is the recommended option for mining sector and government delegation delegates arriving for events. Hovercraft and passenger ferry services are available but have had reliability issues. The overland route via the Lungi Bridge adds 3 to 4 hours. Helicopter transfer must be pre-booked well in advance. The event operations controller coordinates helicopter timing with each delegate's flight arrival, manages waterfront collection at Aberdeen, and then transfers delegates to venue or accommodation.

Venue Security: Aberdeen Peninsula

The Radisson Blu Mammy Yoko Hotel and Country Lodge in the Aberdeen peninsula are the primary international event venues, both positioned in the area of Freetown with the most manageable security environment for international gatherings. Venue security assessment covers access control at hotel entrance (Aberdeen waterfront and approach areas have elevated robbery risk, particularly after dark), vehicle staging, CCTV coverage, and a venue security sweep before each event day. For NGO coordination events, security team configuration is low-profile in alignment with humanitarian sector community perception requirements. Mining sector events with government or investor delegate audiences use a higher-profile access management approach.

Post-Election Unrest Risk

Sierra Leone's post-election violence in 2022 to 2023 demonstrated that political demonstrations can rapidly become confrontational in Freetown. Events coinciding with politically sensitive periods -- election dates, results announcements, political anniversaries -- require specific advance assessment. The FCDO advises a high degree of caution throughout Sierra Leone and specifically notes demonstration risk. Event security planning for Freetown monitors current political conditions against FCDO reporting throughout the planning period and includes contingency plans for shelter-in-place at the event venue if demonstrations affect movement. Source: FCDO Sierra Leone travel advice (2026).

Medical Planning and West Africa Medevac

Choithram Hospital in Freetown provides the best available private care for non-complex conditions. For serious medical events during a conference, the medevac pathway from Lungi International Airport (FNA) to Accra, Ghana (approximately 1 hour by air) or Abidjan, Cote d'Ivoire (approximately 1 hour) is the standard response. The Lungi estuary crossing is incorporated into medevac planning timing. All delegates attending Freetown events must hold insurance covering emergency medical evacuation. A medevac provider on standby for the event duration is required.
