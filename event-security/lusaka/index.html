What our event security covers

Lusaka Event Landscape

Lusaka is Zambia's capital and the commercial hub for the southern Africa copper belt, hosting mining sector investment forums, COMESA regional meetings, development organisation conferences, and bilateral investment events. The Radisson Blu Hotel Lusaka, Intercontinental Lusaka, and the Southern Sun Ridgeway Hotel are the principal conference properties. Zambia's copper sector (the country is the world's second-largest cobalt producer and one of its leading copper producers) attracts significant mining investment event activity from the battery and EV supply chain sectors.

Zambia Security Companies Act Licensing

Private security in Zambia is governed by the Security Companies Act, with security companies licensed through the Zambia Police Service. Individual guards require Zambia Police Service registration. Armed security provision requires additional police authorisation. Our Lusaka event security partners hold Zambia Police Service licensing; documentation is provided to event organisers before deployment.

Venue Security Protocol

Event security in Lusaka applies standard conference security proportionate to the medium risk environment: vehicle access management at the hotel compound entry; x-ray and wand screening for all conference attendees; a pre-registered delegate list with ID verification; and a uniformed and plain-clothes security presence on the conference floor. Lusaka's principal conference hotels have their own security infrastructure; event security supplements it with the delegate transport management and specific advisory on the Lusaka evening crime risk outside the hotel zone.

Opportunistic Crime and Vehicle Security

Lusaka has moderate crime rates by southern African standards, with opportunistic theft, vehicle crime, and occasional carjacking documented in the city centre and outer residential areas. Event delegate transport uses pre-vetted Zambia Police-licensed vehicles with trained drivers. Delegate movements after dark outside the principal hotel zone are actively managed with vetted transport; no unsupported pedestrian movement in the Lusaka CBD after dark is advised.

Kenneth Kaunda Airport Delegate Transfers

Kenneth Kaunda International Airport (LUN) is located approximately 26 kilometres east of central Lusaka. The transfer to the Ridgeway and Great East Road conference hotel zone takes 30 to 50 minutes under normal conditions. Lusaka road infrastructure is adequate; traffic is lighter than comparable-size African capitals. Delegate arrivals at LUN are met inside the terminal with ID-confirmed vetted transport.

Medical Provision and Emergency Planning

Medical facilities in Lusaka are limited by regional standards. Levy Mwanawasa General Hospital and the Eureka Medical Centre provide private care. For complex specialist treatment, medical evacuation to Johannesburg (approximately 2.5 hours by air from LUN) or to Harare (approximately 1.5 hours) is the standard plan. All Lusaka event security assessments document the medical provision plan and evacuation route.
