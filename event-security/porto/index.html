Event Security in Porto

Event security in Porto for Exponor exhibitions, congress centre events, and corporate functions. Venue assessment, licensed close protection, and access control.

Event in Porto? Security planning for Portugal's northern business capital.

Porto is Portugal’s second city and the country’s northern business and cultural capital. The city is an established European conference destination, supported by Exponor in Matosinhos, the Alfandega Porto Congress Centre on the Douro waterfront, and a growing luxury hotel conference infrastructure. Porto is also the commercial gateway to the Douro Valley wine region and hosts significant international trade events in the wine, tourism, and technology sectors.

The security profile

PSP Crime Statistics 2024 confirm Porto as a low-crime city by European standards. The crime that most directly affects international business visitors is opportunistic theft, particularly pickpocketing in the historic Ribeira district and along the main Aliados boulevard where tourist density is high. For corporate event planners, this is primarily a delegate awareness and movement management consideration rather than a structural security threat, but it informs our transfer routing and delegate briefing protocols.

Porto’s international conference profile has grown significantly in recent years, supported by strong air connectivity from OPO airport and the city’s attractive setting. Events at the Alfandega Congress Centre on the Douro waterfront and at Exponor in Matosinhos attract European and international delegates who may be unfamiliar with Porto’s geography and the specific movement considerations in the historic centre.

Planning for Porto events

Lei n. 34/2013 and PSP oversight provide a clear licensing framework for private security in Portugal. Our planning process includes early engagement with venue security teams at Exponor and Alfandega, with attention to credentialing systems for international multi-day conferences. For events incorporating Douro Valley winery visits alongside city conference programming, we build integrated transfer schedules that account for the 60-90 minute journey from Porto to principal Douro Valley venues.

Transfer management between OPO airport and central Porto is approximately 25 minutes in normal traffic conditions. We assess road conditions for each event, with particular attention to A28 and Via de Cintura Interna route options depending on time of day and event timing.

For the wider security picture, see our Porto city security briefing and our event security service.
