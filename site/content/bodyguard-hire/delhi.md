## The PSARA licensing framework

PSARA 2005 and the 2006 Rules set minimum standards for security agency licences, personnel character verification, physical standards, and training. The Delhi state Controlling Authority administers these requirements for all agencies and personnel operating in Delhi and the National Capital Territory. Armed close protection requires additional Police Commissioner authorisation under the Arms Act 1959 and is not a standard commercial offering.
