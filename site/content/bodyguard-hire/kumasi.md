## Operating in Kumasi

The KNUST area, Asokwa, and Tech residential zones are the recommended base areas for corporate principals, carrying lower crime exposure relative to the Adum CBD and Kejetia area. Movement to the Kejetia Market or Central Market requires specific advance planning for crowd management and vehicle positioning. Road movements on Kumasi's congested road network require drivers with defensive driving awareness aligned with the NRSA Ghana 2024 road safety context.
