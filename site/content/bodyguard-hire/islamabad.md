## The Islamabad security environment

FCDO advises a high terrorism threat for Pakistan, including Islamabad. Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan (TTP) and affiliated groups have carried out attacks in Islamabad, including the 2008 Marriott Hotel bombing (54 killed) and subsequent incidents targeting security forces and international hotels. Political demonstrations in Islamabad can turn violent with limited warning, as seen during 2023 protest cycles following political arrests. Kidnapping for ransom targeting foreign nationals and high-profile Pakistanis is documented.

## The PPSRA licensing framework

Private security companies operating in Punjab province, including those serving Islamabad, should hold PPSRA (Punjab Private Security Regulatory Authority) registration. National-level security company licensing also applies under Ministry of Interior regulations. Ask for the PPSRA or relevant provincial registration of any bodyguard company proposed for an Islamabad detail before deployment.
