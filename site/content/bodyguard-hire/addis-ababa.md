The FCDO rates Ethiopia as high risk (2024), with ongoing conflict and insecurity in surrounding regions including Amhara, Oromia, and the post-Tigray corridor, and with Addis Ababa itself subject to protest activity tied to these regional dynamics. The Ministry of Peace licensing framework for private security is less standardised than peers in East Africa; operator due diligence and credential verification are non-negotiable steps before any deployment.

Professional CPO cover in Addis Ababa focuses on the highest-risk moments: the Bole International Airport transfer, predictable hotel-to-office movements, and periods of elevated political activity. The Bole Road and Kazanchis districts are the primary operating zones for senior corporate and diplomatic principal protection.
