## The Guadalajara security environment

CJNG is headquartered in Jalisco state, of which Guadalajara is the capital. CJNG is assessed as one of Mexico's most powerful criminal organisations. Direct targeting of international business visitors is not CJNG's primary activity, but territorial enforcement actions affect the wider metropolitan area. Express kidnapping (paseo millonario) using unlicensed taxis operates in Guadalajara as in Mexico City. Armed robbery and vehicle crime are documented. Source: Mexican National Security Commission; InSight Crime Mexico analysis, 2025.
