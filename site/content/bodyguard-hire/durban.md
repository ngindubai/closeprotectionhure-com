Durban is South Africa's 3rd largest city and the busiest container port in Africa. SAPS Annual Crime Report 2023/24 identifies eThekwini Municipality as among the highest-crime metro areas in South Africa. Carjacking, armed robbery, and vehicle crime are at elevated levels across the metro. Professional PSIRA-registered close protection with carjacking-aware vehicle protocols is the appropriate baseline for senior executive and HNWI principals visiting Durban, with Umhlanga as the recommended operating base.

## Operating in Durban

Umhlanga provides the lowest-risk operating base in the Durban metro, with direct N2 access to King Shaka International Airport and La Lucia Ridge business parks. The Durban CBD should be approached as an elevated-risk zone with vetted transport and pre-planned movements only.

## Licensing and operator selection

All close protection operators in Durban must hold PSIRA registration under PSIRA 2001. Armed operators additionally require Firearms Control Act 60/2000 certification.
