## The Argentine regulatory framework

Private security in Buenos Aires operates under a provincial and municipal licensing system (Ley 12.297 for Buenos Aires Province; separate municipal regime for CABA) rather than a unified national framework. Individual guard credentialing and company registration are the verifiable compliance measures. Federal oversight is provided by the Ministerio de Seguridad.

## The Buenos Aires CPO threat environment
