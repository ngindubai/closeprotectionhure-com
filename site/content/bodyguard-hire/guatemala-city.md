## DIGESSP licensing and Guatemala compliance

CPOs must hold DIGESSP company licences and individual DIGESSP credentials under Ministry of Interior authorisation. Both compliant and non-compliant operators exist in Guatemala's large private security market; credential verification is essential. Armed operations require DIGESSP armed authorisation and specific firearm licensing. Foreign operators work through DIGESSP-licensed Guatemalan partners.
