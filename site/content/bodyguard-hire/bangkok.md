## The regulatory framework in Bangkok

Thailand's private security sector operates under a Ministry of Interior and DOPA registration framework, with Royal Thai Police coordination for armed operations. There is no single licensing statute equivalent to the UK Private Security Industry Act; operators should be vetted for company registration, insurance, and RTP compliance. Most commercial CPO work in Bangkok is unarmed; armed cover requires specific authorisation.
