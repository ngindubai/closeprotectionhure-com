Licensing follows the standard German pattern, Sec 34a Gewerbeordnung and the Bewachungsverordnung, registration through the Stuttgart Ordnungsamt and IHK Region Stuttgart, and firearms authorisation that is rarely granted, so deployments are unarmed.
