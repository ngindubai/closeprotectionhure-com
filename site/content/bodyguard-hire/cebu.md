Cebu is the Philippines' second-largest urban economy, drawing corporate principals across manufacturing, logistics, IT outsourcing, and tourism sectors. ## Operating in Cebu

Cebu Business Park and IT Park are the primary corporate operating zones, with established hotel and commercial infrastructure and a lower crime profile relative to the wider city. Movement to Colon Street, Carbon Market, or provincial areas outside the city requires specific advance planning. Typhoon-season operations require additional contingency planning, particularly for principals with fixed departure windows at Mactan-Cebu International Airport.
