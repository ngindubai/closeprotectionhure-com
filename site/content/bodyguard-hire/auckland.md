## The New Zealand regulatory framework

The Private Security Personnel and Private Investigators Act 2010 (PSPPIA) provides individual CoA certification through MBIE, with operating company licensing as the institutional framework. The CoA is publicly verifiable, making New Zealand one of the more transparent private security markets in the Asia-Pacific region.
