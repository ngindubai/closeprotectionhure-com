## The SIA licensing framework

The Security Industry Authority (SIA) was established under the Private Security Industry Act 2001 as the statutory regulator for the UK private security industry. The SIA Close Protection licence is mandatory for commercial bodyguard and CPO work in England, Wales, and Scotland. Licence details are publicly verifiable. The licensing framework includes minimum training standards (Level 3 Award in Close Protection from a regulated provider), First Aid certification, and enhanced criminal record vetting. Operating companies that place CPOs must also hold an SIA Approved Contractor Scheme (ACS) accreditation in most commercial contexts.
