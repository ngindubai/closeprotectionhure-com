Nantes is the capital of the Loire Atlantique region and an established hub for the French aerospace and naval sectors. Airbus and Thales maintain operations in the Nantes and Bouguenais zones, and Chantiers de l'Atlantique, the historic STX shipyard at Saint-Nazaire 60km west, generates executive and government-delegation traffic for the naval and offshore energy industries. The FCDO France Travel Advice (2024) notes the national Vigipirate Urgence Attentat posture; French private security operates under Loi 83-629 and CNAPS licensing oversight, with the US Embassy Paris (+33 1 43 12 22 22) and British Embassy Paris (+33 1 44 51 31 00) providing consular coverage for the region.
