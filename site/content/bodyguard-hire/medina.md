This page covers how close protection hire works in Saudi Arabia's holy city, what a Saudi-licensed operator under Council of Ministers Resolution No. 58 provides across a day of movements, and how quickly cover is arranged. Non-Muslim personnel are restricted from certain areas, so hire is built around Saudi-licensed local operators, scaled to the itinerary.

CPO cover concentrates on the hotel district around the Prophet's Mosque, including the Anwar Al Madinah Movenpick and the Marriott Madinah, and on pilgrimage-logistics and construction-sector corporate sites. Movement planning distinguishes permitted areas from the restricted Haram. The airport (MED) transfer, 12km from the city, is the highest-exposure moment and is handled with confirmed inside-terminal collection and route-planned transfer.
