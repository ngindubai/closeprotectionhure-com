## The Egyptian licensing framework

Law 394 of 1954, as modified, and MoI regulatory practice govern all private security licensing in Egypt. Companies must hold MoI licences; individual personnel must be MoI-approved. Foreign operators must work through an Egyptian-licensed partner. Armed close protection requires specific MoI weapons authorisation beyond the standard company and personnel licence.
