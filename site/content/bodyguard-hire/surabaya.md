Surabaya is Indonesia's 2nd largest city and a major industrial and port hub in East Java. FCDO Indonesia 2026 rates Java at Level 1 and the city presents a moderate overall risk profile. Road traffic safety (NTMC Indonesia 2024: high road fatality rate) is the primary daily operational challenge. Polri BUJP-permitted close protection officers, trained in defensive driving and road safety awareness, provide the appropriate baseline for corporate and HNWI principals operating in Surabaya.

## Operating in Surabaya

The Ciputra World corridor and Wiyung/Pakuwon residential areas form the primary operating base for senior executive and HNWI principals. Surabaya CBD and Gubeng carry moderate risk. Market areas require advance planning and vetted transport. Protest monitoring is integrated into advance planning for all Surabaya assignments.
