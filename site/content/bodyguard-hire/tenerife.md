Hiring a bodyguard on Tenerife is typically about tourist-area petty theft and mountain and coastal road driving rather than violent crime. This page covers how close protection hire works in the Canary Islands, what an SES-licensed officer does across a day of movements, and how quickly cover is arranged. Armed work is uncommon for commercial clients, so hire pairs an unarmed licensed officer with secure transport, scaled to the itinerary rather than a fixed contract.

CPO cover concentrates on the Costa Adeje resorts including the Gran Hotel Bahia del Duque and the Ritz-Carlton Abama, corporate meetings in Santa Cruz, and golf and marina venues. With two airports serving the island, Tenerife North (TFN) for business and Tenerife South (TFS) for tourism, transfers are the highest-exposure moments and are handled with confirmed inside-terminal collection and route-planned transfer.
