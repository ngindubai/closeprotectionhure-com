## The Niamey security environment

Niger's CNSP junta suspended the constitution and expelled French and US military forces following the July 2023 coup. JNIM and IS-Sahel operate along Niger's borders with Mali and Burkina Faso; the Tillabery region is a major conflict zone. Niamey itself has not experienced the frequency of attacks seen in border areas, but the withdrawal of western counter-terrorism forces has reduced available security intelligence and the city's threat environment has deteriorated. The US State Department maintains a Level 4 Do Not Travel advisory for Niger. Source: FCDO Niger travel advice, 2025.
