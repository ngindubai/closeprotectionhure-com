The terrorism threat is elevated following the November 2020 attack, and the BVT maintains ongoing counter-terrorism monitoring. Bodyguard hire in Vienna is governed by the Austrian Gewerbeordnung (GewO), with all commercial CPO personnel required to hold the Befähigungsprüfung and operate through a licensed Bewachungsgewerbe company.

## The Austrian licensing framework

The Gewerbeordnung section 94 item 73 establishes the legal basis for all Bewachungsgewerbe (security guarding) activities in Austria, including close protection. The competency examination (Befähigungsprüfung) covers both technical security knowledge and Austrian law. Companies must hold a district-authority-issued licence. Austria does not permit private security personnel to carry firearms; all CPO work is conducted on an unarmed basis.
