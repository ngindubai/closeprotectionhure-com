## The Malaysian licensing framework

PSA 2017 and the 2020 Licensing Regulations replaced the earlier Uniform Bodies (Discipline) Act and established clear licensing requirements for security personnel and companies. PDRM is the licensing authority. Armed close protection requires additional PDRM authorisation beyond the standard licence. All standard CPO work in KL is unarmed.
