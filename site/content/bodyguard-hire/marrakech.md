Marrakech is Morocco's primary destination for HNWI and high-value corporate travel, drawing principals for hospitality, investment, and cultural visits. FCDO Morocco 2026 rates the city at Level 2 and OSAC Morocco 2024 records low violent crime overall, but the Medina, Djemaa el-Fna, and major souk areas present specific petty crime and crowd management challenges that benefit from professional close protection planning.

## Operating in Marrakech

Gueliz and Hivernage are the primary operating bases for corporate principals, with lower crime exposure and established international hotel infrastructure. Palmeraie villa estates offer HNWI privacy but require advance planning for approach road access and estate entry protocols. Movement into the Medina requires specific advance work: route planning through the derb network, vehicle drop-off and pick-up point pre-selection, and CPO positioning in crowd-dense areas.

## Licensing and operator selection

All close protection operators in Marrakech must hold Direction Generale de la Surete Nationale authorisation under Law 17-89. The DGSN register is publicly accessible and verified before operator engagement.
