## The Tripoli security environment

Tripoli is governed in practice by competing militia groups rather than a unified state security apparatus. The Government of National Unity (GNU) holds nominal international recognition but does not exercise exclusive control. Militias including the Rada SDF, Nawasi Brigade, and others operate across the city. Inter-militia clashes, including the 2018 and 2019 fighting, involved heavy weapons in residential and commercial areas. Kidnapping of foreign nationals for ransom is documented, targeting oil sector workers, diplomats, and NGO personnel. The US State Department maintains a Level 4 Do Not Travel advisory for Libya. Source: FCDO Libya travel advice, 2025.
