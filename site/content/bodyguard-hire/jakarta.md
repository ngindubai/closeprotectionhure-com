## The Indonesian regulatory framework

POLRI regulation under Peraturan Kapolri No. 24 Tahun 2007 provides company-level licensing with Baintelkam POLRI oversight. Company registration verification and personnel vetting standards are the primary client due-diligence measures. The market includes internationally affiliated operators with high standards that represent the appropriate selection for corporate principal protection.

## The Jakarta CPO profile

Jakarta CPO engagements are dominated by vehicle-based transfer planning through an extreme congestion environment, SCBD and Menteng district coverage for corporate and diplomatic assignments, Soekarno-Hatta airport transfer operations, and political awareness monitoring during elevated-risk periods.
