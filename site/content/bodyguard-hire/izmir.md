Izmir is Turkey's 3rd largest city and primary Aegean port, with a significant trade, hospitality, and manufacturing economy. FCDO Turkey 2026 rates Izmir at Level 2 (exercise normal precautions), placing it at a substantially lower operational risk than southeast Turkey and broadly comparable to other major European port cities. TNP-permitted close protection with terrorism awareness, demonstration monitoring, and venue pre-inspection capability is the appropriate baseline for senior executive and HNWI principals visiting Izmir.

## Operating in Izmir

Alsancak and Konak form the primary corporate and hotel district. Karsiyaka and Bornova provide the lower-risk residential base. Cesme is the HNWI coastal leisure destination, requiring road transfer planning from the city. All principal movements are pre-planned with route survey and demonstration monitoring.
