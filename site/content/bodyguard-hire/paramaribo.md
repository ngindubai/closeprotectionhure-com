## Ministry of Justice and Police licensing

CPOs in Suriname must hold Ministry of Justice and Police authorisation. The regulatory framework is less developed than in neighbouring states; independent credential verification before deployment is essential. Foreign operators work through Ministry-authorised Surinamese partners.
