This page covers how close protection hire works in Greece, what a Greek Police licensed officer does across a day of movements, and how quickly cover is arranged. Armed assignments require Ministry of Public Order approval and are uncommon, so hire pairs a licensed officer with secure transport, scaled to the itinerary rather than a fixed contract.

CPO cover concentrates on the Aristotle Square and waterfront hotel corridor, the TIF grounds during exhibition periods, and the Toumba district for events, with monitoring of the university and Kamara areas for demonstration activity. Makedonia Airport (SKG) transfers, 13km from the city, are the highest-exposure moments and are handled with confirmed inside-terminal collection and route-planned transfer.
