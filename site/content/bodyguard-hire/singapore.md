## The PLRD framework

Singapore's Police Licensing and Regulatory Department (PLRD) administers the private security licensing framework under the Private Security Industry Act (Cap. 250A). Security agencies must hold PLRD licences; individual security officers must be PLRD-registered. Singapore's regulatory standard is high, and PLRD enforcement is active. Operating companies are selected for full PLRD compliance as a non-negotiable requirement.
