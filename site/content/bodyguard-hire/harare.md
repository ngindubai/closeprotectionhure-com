Zimbabwe's economy has experienced hyperinflation, currency collapses, and multiple monetary reforms. Economic instability creates social tension and unpredictable operating conditions. ## Zimbabwe private security licensing

Zimbabwe's private security sector operates under the Private Investigators and Security Guards Act, with companies and individual guards required to register with the Zimbabwe Republic Police (ZRP). Ask for the ZRP registration of any bodyguard company proposed for a Harare detail and confirm it through reference checks with known international operators before engagement.
