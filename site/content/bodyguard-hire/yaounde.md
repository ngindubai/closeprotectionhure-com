## MINAT licensing and Cameroon compliance

CPOs must hold MINAT (Ministry of Territorial Administration) authorisation and individual credentials. Both compliant and non-compliant operators exist in Cameroon's security market; verification before deployment is essential. Regional travel toward conflict-affected areas requires additional MINAT coordination. Foreign operators work through MINAT-authorised Cameroonian partners.
