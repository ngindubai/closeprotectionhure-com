## The Guayaquil security environment

Guayaquil is assessed as high risk. The security environment has deteriorated significantly since 2022, driven by the expansion of Colombian cartel-linked criminal organisations into Ecuador's port infrastructure. Kidnapping, armed robbery, gang violence, and port-related extortion have all increased. The January 2024 security crisis, in which Ecuador declared an internal armed conflict, was significantly driven by Guayaquil-area gang dynamics. The US State Department Ecuador advisory is Level 3 (Reconsider Travel). Source: FCDO Ecuador travel advice (2026); US State Department Ecuador advisory Level 3 (2026).
