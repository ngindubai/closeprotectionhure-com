## The Malaysian regulatory framework for CPOs

PALB licensing under the Private Agencies Act 1971 is the regulatory baseline. The Royal Malaysian Police maintains operational oversight. Individual personnel are registered through their employing company, making company-level due diligence the primary client verification responsibility.
