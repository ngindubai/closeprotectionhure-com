## The Spanish regulatory framework for CPOs

Ley 5/2014 creates two relevant licence categories: the general vigilante de seguridad privada and the specialist escolta privado. Both hold a TIP (Tarjeta de Identidad Profesional) from the Ministerio del Interior. The escolta privado is the legally correct designation for close protection work. TIP details are verifiable through the Interior Ministry's licensing database.
