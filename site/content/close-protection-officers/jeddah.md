## The GASC regulatory framework

Saudi Arabia's General Authority for Security Companies (GASC) is the Ministry of Interior body responsible for licensing all commercial security operators, both companies and individuals. GASC licensing is verifiable, and verification is a required step before engaging any Jeddah CPO provider. Saudisation requirements mean that the workforce composition of licensed companies must meet minimum Saudi national staffing ratios.
