## The Law 5188 framework

Turkey's Law 5188 on Private Security Services (Ozel Guvenlik Hizmetlerine Dair Kanun, 2004) established the national framework for licensing private security personnel and companies. Individual CPOs hold an Ozel Guvenlik Kimlik Karti issued by the Provincial Security Directorate; operating companies hold separate operating licences under the same law. The Ministry of Interior oversees compliance. Both the company licence and the individual CPO card are verifiable and should be confirmed before engaging any operator.
