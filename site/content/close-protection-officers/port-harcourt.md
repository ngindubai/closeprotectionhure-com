## The NSCDC regulatory framework

Nigeria Security and Civil Defence Corps (NSCDC) licensing under the Private Guard Companies Act (Cap P29) is the regulatory standard for private security in Port Harcourt. Both operating companies and individual armed personnel require NSCDC registration. The NSCDC register is publicly accessible, and verification before engagement is the required first step. Operators without current NSCDC licensing create significant legal liability for the engaging organisation.
