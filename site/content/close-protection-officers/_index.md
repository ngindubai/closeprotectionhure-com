A close protection officer (CPO) is a licensed, trained security professional distinct from a general security guard. CPO licensing in most jurisdictions requires specific qualifications, practical scenario training, and vetting through national police or regulatory authorities. The city pages below cover the CPO licensing regimes, typical operational profiles, and service capabilities for the cities where corporate and HNWI clients most frequently request qualified personal protection.
