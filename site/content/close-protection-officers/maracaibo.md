Enforcement of standards is inconsistent and verification of licences through official channels is unreliable. International quality certification standards cannot be assumed for locally licensed operators, and CPO firms operating in this environment should conduct their own capability assessments of any local partner, including verification of training records, references from other international organisations, and direct assessment of the partner's vehicles, communications equipment, and incident history. Infrastructure collapse is a material operational factor: sustained power outages disable CCTV systems, electronic access controls, and communication infrastructure across large parts of the city for extended periods. The oil production zone in the Maracaibo Basin operates largely within company-controlled compounds and is a more controlled environment than the city itself, but even compound perimeters require active security management."
