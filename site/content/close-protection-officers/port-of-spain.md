The central business district, the St Clair and Westmoorings residential and commercial zones, and the Point Lisas industrial corridor to the south form the primary operating environment for international corporate and energy sector principals. The TTPS and PSIRA T&T govern private security licensing, and the gang crime and firearms violence documented in the FCDO Trinidad and Tobago advisory (2025) defines the risk profile that all CPO operations must address.
