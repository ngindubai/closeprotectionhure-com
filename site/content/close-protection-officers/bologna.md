Choosing a close protection officer in Bologna starts with the paperwork, and it starts there because the paperwork is where most avoidable problems originate. A Prefettura licence covers the company. A Questura authorisation covers the individual officer standing in front of you. They are not the same document, and a client who checks only one has not actually completed the check.
