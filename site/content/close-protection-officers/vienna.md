## The Austrian regulatory framework for CPOs

Bewachungsgewerbe licensing under the Gewerbeordnung 1994 and Personenschutz qualification through WKO-recognised training provide the regulatory baseline. DSGVO (Austrian GDPR) compliance applies to counter-surveillance and personal data processing activities. The WKO Fachverband registry provides the verification reference for licensed operators.

## The Vienna CPO profile

Vienna CPO engagements focus on the Innere Stadt and Ring corridor for diplomatic and hotel venue work, the Vienna International Centre for UNOV and IAEA engagements, and Vienna International Airport for arrival and departure transfer operations.
