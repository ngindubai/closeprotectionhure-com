## The Cambodian CPO licensing environment

Cambodia's Ministry of Interior administers private security licensing under the Law on the Management of Private Bodyguards and Security Guards. The framework is less developed than counterpart systems in Southeast Asian hubs such as Singapore, but Ministry of Interior registration is the applicable standard and verification is required before any operator is engaged. The quality of licensed operators varies considerably: documented close protection training and verifiable deployment history are the appropriate screening criteria beyond the basic registration requirement.
