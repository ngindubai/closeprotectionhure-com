## The Punjab security licensing framework

Punjab's Home Department administers security company licensing under the Security Companies (Regulation) Act, a provincial-level regulatory system separate from the federal framework. Individual armed CPOs require Pakistan Arms Ordinance weapons licensing. Verification of both the company's Punjab Home Department operating licence and the individual CPO's weapons documentation is a required step before any engagement in Lahore begins.
