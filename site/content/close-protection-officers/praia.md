Praia is the capital of Cape Verde, an Atlantic island archipelago that sits roughly 570 kilometres off the coast of Senegal. Cape Verde is a functioning democracy with a stable political environment, no armed group presence, and low levels of street crime relative to many of its West African neighbours. The US State Department rates it Level 1 (2026) and the FCDO recommends standard precautions -- making it one of the most benign operating environments on the continent. CPO requirements in Praia therefore arise primarily from principal profile rather than environmental threat. High-net-worth individuals, public figures, and senior executives may benefit from professional close protection regardless of destination risk level, and the discreet, low-profile format appropriate to Cape Verde's relaxed environment is exactly what specialist operators provide.

Deployments in Praia are typically centred on Nelson Mandela International Airport (RAI) arrivals, transfers to hotels or private villas in the city and along the Santiago Island coast, and coverage during investment meetings, government visits, or leisure activities. For principals continuing from Cape Verde to higher-risk destinations on the West African mainland, we provide coordinated cross-deployment planning to ensure continuity of cover.
