## The Thai regulatory framework for CPOs

The Private Security Business Act B.E. 2558 (2015) establishes company-level licensing as the regulatory baseline for Bangkok CPO operations. Royal Thai Police oversight and approved vetting processes govern personnel standards. Due diligence focuses on the operating company rather than an individual public licence, requiring clients to engage through operators with demonstrable vetting standards and documented operational history in Bangkok.

## The Bangkok CPO profile

Bangkok CPO engagements operate at a consistently low visible profile in corporate and hospitality environments. Counter-surveillance awareness, political monitoring, and airport transfer capability are standard operational components. Pre-advance at the principal's venues is built into every multi-day Bangkok engagement.
