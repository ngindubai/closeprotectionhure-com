## The MINAT licensing framework

Cameroon's Ministry of Territorial Administration (MINAT) authorises private security companies and individual operators. The framework has been updated in recent years, but enforcement capacity is more limited than counterpart systems in Nigeria or southern Africa. MINAT authorisation is the applicable regulatory standard; beyond the licence check, screening of operator quality through documented close protection training records and verifiable deployment history is the appropriate additional due diligence for a Douala engagement.
