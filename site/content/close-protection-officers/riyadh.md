## The Saudi regulatory framework for private security

Private security in Saudi Arabia is regulated through the Ministry of Interior, which licences operating companies and sets the standards for personnel vetting and training. The Saudi private security market has developed primarily to serve the Kingdom's large diplomatic, energy, and corporate sectors. Foreign security companies operating in Saudi Arabia must do so through locally licensed partners.
