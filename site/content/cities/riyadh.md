Riyadh is Saudi Arabia's capital and a major centre for international business, particularly in the energy and financial sectors. The security environment is shaped more by regulatory and cultural factors than by conventional crime.

## The security landscape

Street crime in Riyadh is lower than in most P1 cities. The primary security concerns are terrorism (from regional extremist groups), regional conflict spillover (missile/drone attacks from Yemen), and the strict legal environment that can create unexpected consequences for uninformed visitors.
