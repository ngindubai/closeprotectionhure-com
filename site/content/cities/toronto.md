Toronto is Canada's largest city and financial capital, home to the country's major banks, a significant technology sector, and one of North America's most diverse business environments. The Greater Toronto Area hosts major conferences, government summits, and a large proportion of Canada's high-net-worth population.

## The security landscape

Toronto operates within Canada's stable governance framework. It is not a high-threat environment by global standards. The relevant security considerations are targeted crime (vehicle theft, HNWI residential targeting), foreign state interference for specific profiles, and the corporate espionage risk for technology and finance sector executives.

## Protective security in Toronto

Ontario's regulated security sector provides licensed, qualified close protection professionals. The market is mature and familiar with both corporate executive protection and HNWI personal security requirements.

For most visitors, Toronto requires standard business travel precautions. For those with elevated threat profiles or specific concerns, professional security support is readily available through Ontario-licensed operators.
