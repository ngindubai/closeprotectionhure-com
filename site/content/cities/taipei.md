Taipei is the corporate, financial, and government centre of Taiwan, with business activity concentrated in the Xinyi CBD, Da'an diplomatic district, and Zhongzheng government quarter. The ambient security environment is low-risk by any international measure: Taiwan NPA Crime Statistics 2024 confirm low violent crime, with residual risk limited to minor petty crime in nightlife areas such as Ximending. The distinguishing planning factor for Taipei is the cross-strait strategic context, noted by the FCDO (2026) as a contingency and continuity planning consideration rather than a routine personal-safety concern. The FCDO advises normal precautions for Taiwan.

For principals requiring close protection in Taipei, all operators must hold NPA-MOI approval under the Private Security Services Industry Act 2009.
