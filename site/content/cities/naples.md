Naples is southern Italy's largest city and commercial centre, a hub for manufacturing, logistics, and the cultural and luxury tourism sector. It sits at the gateway to the Amalfi Coast, Capri, and the broader Campania region, making it a common destination for both corporate visits and high-net-worth leisure.

## The security landscape

Naples operates within Italy's functional legal and security framework. Carabinieri and Polizia are present and operational. Emergency services function. The elevated crime rates are real but the city is not lawless.

The organised crime dimension is the differentiator from other Italian cities. The Camorra is a structured criminal organisation with genuine presence in commercial and construction sectors. For business visitors, this is primarily a due diligence consideration rather than a physical security risk.

## Protective security in Naples

For HNWI clients visiting the Amalfi Coast or Capri with Naples as a gateway, close protection and vetted transport coordination is appropriate given the combination of high-value profile and unfamiliar environment. For standard corporate visits, a vetted driver and situational awareness are the practical requirements.
