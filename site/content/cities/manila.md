Manila is the economic centre of the Philippines and a major regional business hub. The kidnap-for-ransom threat, combined with general crime levels and political instability, creates a security environment that demands professional management for corporate travellers.

## The security landscape

Kidnap-for-ransom is the defining threat. The Philippines consistently ranks among the top countries globally for kidnapping incidents. While much of the activity is concentrated in Mindanao, Metro Manila experiences incidents targeting both foreign nationals and wealthy local business figures.

Armed robbery is common across the metro area. Traffic congestion is among the worst in Asia, creating prolonged vulnerability during ground movements.

## Our operations in Manila

Operators hold PNP and SOSIA licensing with documented Metro Manila experience. Armed close protection is available and recommended for higher-profile assignments. Security drivers maintain real-time communication with our operations centre. For corporate travellers, we recommend operating primarily within the Makati and BGC business districts with security support for all movements outside these areas.
