Ho Chi Minh City is Vietnam's commercial engine and the primary destination for corporate investment, manufacturing, and regional headquarters activity in Vietnam. The city is home to a large and established expatriate community across finance, manufacturing, technology, and the NGO sector.

## The security landscape

HCMC presents a relatively benign security environment for business travel by regional standards. There is no significant terrorism risk and political unrest is uncommon. The primary security concerns are street crime (particularly motorbike snatching), traffic safety, and the need for business due diligence.

For senior executives and HNWI clients, the relevant consideration is the absence of strong protective infrastructure relative to more developed markets. Emergency response, medical care, and legal support all require advance planning.

## Operating in Ho Chi Minh City

Most corporate visitors find that standard security precautions are sufficient for HCMC. Reputable accommodation in District 1, 2, or 7, verified transport, and awareness of the street crime environment are the practical requirements.

For those on extended assignments or with specific threat concerns, Vietnamese security providers can deliver close protection with appropriate local knowledge and regulatory compliance.
