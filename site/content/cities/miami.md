Miami is the financial capital of Latin America in commercial terms and a major hub for wealth, corporate activity, and high-profile events. It is home to significant Latin American business operations, the regional headquarters of international banks, and a large HNWI population.

## The security landscape

Miami presents a bifurcated security environment. The business and high-end residential districts are broadly safe with active law enforcement. The elevated crime areas exist but are geographically distinct from where most corporate and HNWI activity occurs.

The primary security consideration for most clients in Miami is less about ambient threat and more about the specific risk from wealth targeting. Miami's concentration of public wealth display creates an environment where high-value individuals attract disproportionate criminal interest at specific times and venues.

## Protective security in Miami

Miami's close protection market is well-developed. Licensed, experienced operators are available for the full range of protective security services. The event calendar (Art Basel, Formula 1, major music events) creates peaks in security demand that require advance booking of quality resource.
