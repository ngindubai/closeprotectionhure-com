Amsterdam is the Netherlands' commercial capital, home to major multinationals (Shell, Philips, ASML, ING, Heineken), a significant financial sector, and a large HNWI community. The nearby Hague hosts major international legal and diplomatic institutions. Schiphol is one of Europe's primary aviation hubs.

## The security landscape

Amsterdam operates within the Netherlands' functional legal and security framework. Day-to-day risks are primarily petty crime and the specific hazards of navigating a dense, bicycle-dominated city. The terrorism threat and, more recently, the risk of politically-motivated violence around specific events, are the elevated considerations for security planning.

The November 2024 violence was a reminder that Amsterdam's orderly appearance does not make it immune to rapid escalation in specific circumstances.

## Protective security in Amsterdam

The Netherlands' Wpbr-regulated security sector provides professional close protection services for corporate and HNWI clients. Operators are familiar with both Amsterdam's specific environment and the demands of international clients transiting through Schiphol or attending events in The Hague.
