Washington DC is the US political capital and home to the federal government, Congress, the Supreme Court, and the headquarters of major regulatory agencies, lobbying organisations, policy institutes, and international financial institutions. Major corporate and foreign government engagement with the US government takes place in DC.

## The security landscape

DC presents a specific security environment shaped by its political function. The threat environment for politically-engaged principals is genuinely elevated relative to a standard commercial city. Domestic extremist threats targeting political figures, foreign state interest in government-adjacent activities, and the protest environment all require attention.

For standard corporate visits (board meetings, regulatory consultations, conference attendance), DC operates as a broadly functional US city with manageable security requirements.

## Protective security in DC

DC's close protection market is well-developed and experienced with the specific demands of the political environment. Operators understand federal agency coordination, the demonstration environment, and the specific norms of protecting corporate principals in politically sensitive engagements.
