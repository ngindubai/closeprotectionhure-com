Colombo is Sri Lanka's commercial capital and primary business hub. The city's strategic location in the Indian Ocean and the development of the Colombo Port City project has attracted significant international investment interest, particularly from infrastructure, logistics, and financial sectors.

## The security landscape

Colombo's security environment is shaped by two distinct events: the 2019 Easter Sunday bombings, which placed international security attention on Sri Lanka's vulnerability to terrorism, and the 2022 political crisis, which demonstrated the fragility of the country's political stability.

Both have required significant security system responses. Hotel security is substantially more rigorous post-2019. Political monitoring has become a standard element of operational planning for those on extended assignments.

## Operating in Colombo

For corporate visits, standard security measures are appropriate: vetted accommodation with good physical security, pre-arranged transport, and monitoring of the current political and economic situation. For infrastructure project visits outside Colombo, route and site assessment is the starting point.
