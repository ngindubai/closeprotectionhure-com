Brussels South Charleroi Airport (BSCA), Ryanair's continental base and Belgium's second-busiest airport, handled a record 10.5 million passengers in 2024. That volume shapes the collection protocol here: peak-period terminal and access-road congestion is planned around with additional transit time, not treated as an exception.

The transfer service covers the short run into Gosselies, home to Sonaca's aerospace operations and the A6K digital-innovation hub, both based in the same area of the city as the airport, as well as the roughly 46km corridor north to Brussels for principals whose itinerary spans both cities.
