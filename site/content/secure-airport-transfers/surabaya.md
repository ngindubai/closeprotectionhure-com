Surabaya is Indonesia's second-largest city and the primary port and commercial hub of East Java, serving a substantial manufacturing, logistics, and trading economy. Juanda International Airport connects Surabaya to Indonesian domestic routes and international destinations across Asia. The transfer environment is moderate-risk by Southeast Asian standards, but the Waru corridor congestion and seasonal flooding create specific route management requirements that a standard taxi or ride-share service does not address.

## The SUB Juanda transfer context

POLRI Police Regulation No. 4/2020 and Polda Jawa Timur licensing are the baseline regulatory checks for private security transport in Surabaya. The primary transfer variables are route timing on the Waru corridor and flooding contingency during the wet season.

## What secure transfers cover

A Surabaya secure airport transfer covers: inside-hall collection at SUB by a Polda Jawa Timur-licensed driver; operations controller tracking from SUB to destination; Waru corridor real-time route management; seasonal flooding contingency routing; and return transfers on the same terms.
