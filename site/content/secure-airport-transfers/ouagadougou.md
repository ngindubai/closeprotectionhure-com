Ouagadougou Airport (OUA) is approximately 5km from the city centre. The compact airport-to-city distance does not reduce the security requirement in an environment where FCDO advises against all travel and the US State Department rates Burkina Faso at Level 4. JNIM and IS Sahel Province have conducted attacks in Ouagadougou itself; transfer requires specialist Sahel operators, not standard corporate transport.

## The OUA arrival environment

Ouagadougou serves essential operations arrivals: UN agency staff, diplomatic missions, mining companies with pre-existing infrastructure, and journalists operating in the Sahel. The junta's expulsion of French forces and engagement with Africa Corps has fundamentally altered the operational security architecture. All arrivals require host-nation coordination and confirmed extraction planning before landing.

## What an Ouagadougou secure transfer covers

Advance coordination with the OUA security authority; specialist Sahel operator-managed collection; operations controller oversight from OUA to the confirmed compound or hotel in the Koulouba or Ouaga 2000 zone; pre-confirmed secondary vehicle and routing; and pre-documented extraction protocol to Accra or Abidjan.
