Incheon International Airport (ICN) is one of the world's largest airport complexes, 52km west of central Seoul, serving as the primary international gateway for corporate and HNWI principals travelling to South Korea. Seoul Metropolitan Government Traffic Report 2024 records sustained peak-hour congestion on the Gyeongbu Expressway approach routes, making pre-planned, operations-controller-monitored transfers the standard for principals requiring controlled, verified collection. Gimpo International (GMP) provides a faster 18km ground option for domestic and short-haul arrivals.

## The Seoul airport transfer environment

The Gyeongbu Expressway and Olympic Boulevard are the principal corridors from Incheon to central Seoul and Gangnam, with Seoul TOPIS real-time traffic data incorporated into all transfer route planning. AREX non-stop rail covers Incheon to Seoul Station in 43 minutes and is noted for unescorted principals. All vehicle transfers include inside-terminal name-board collection and an operations controller tracking the transfer from aircraft block-off to accommodation arrival.

## Korean National Police Agency regulated operators

Transfer operators hold current KNPA registration under the Security Services Industry Act 2001. Clients may request verification of KNPA registration status before any engagement.
