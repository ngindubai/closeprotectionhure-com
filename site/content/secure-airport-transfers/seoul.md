Incheon International Airport is the principal gateway for corporate and HNWI arrivals into Seoul, and the 60km corridor from ICN to Gangnam represents the most common high-exposure movement for a principal on a Seoul itinerary. NPA Crime Statistics 2024 record very low violent crime in Seoul overall, but the airport kerb zone and unvetted transport market present the standard risks of unconfirmed driver identity and untracked movement. ## The Seoul airport transfer environment

The Gyeongbu Expressway and the Incheon International Airport Expressway (Route 130) connect ICN to Gangnam and Jongno. Peak congestion during 07:00-09:30 and 17:30-20:00 can add 30-40 minutes to a standard off-peak transfer time. Seoul Metropolitan Government 2024 traffic data is used to plan timing and alternative routes. For principals arriving at Gimpo (GMP) on domestic or short-haul services, the Olympic Expressway corridor is pre-surveyed with a Gangseo-daero alternative.

## Licensing and verification in Seoul

All secure transfer operators in South Korea must hold Guard Services Act (Gyeongbieop) registration through the National Police Agency of Korea. Individual drivers require NPA-accredited training certification. The NPA register is publicly available and verified before any driver engagement.
