## The Kolkata airport transfer environment

VIP Road (NH-12) is the standard inbound route. Peak congestion at 08:00-10:00 and 17:30-20:00 and hartal-related road closures are the two primary planning variables. Kolkata Police Traffic Department 2024 data informs route selection; EM Bypass and AJC Bose Road alternatives are pre-mapped for both congestion and hartal conditions.
