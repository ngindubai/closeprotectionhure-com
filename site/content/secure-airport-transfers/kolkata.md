Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU) is 17km from central Kolkata, with transfer times of 35-55 minutes outside peak periods on the VIP Road and Eastern Metropolitan Bypass corridors. Kolkata Traffic Police 2024 records heavy peak-hour congestion on VIP Road and at the Ultadanga Bridge, and notes unlicensed transport solicitation at the CCU kerbside as a persistent issue for international arrivals.

## The Kolkata airport transfer environment

VIP Road and the Eastern Metropolitan Bypass provide the primary route from CCU to Park Street, Salt Lake City, and New Town (Rajarhat) destinations. Monsoon flooding (June-September) requires route pre-survey and alternative routing via the EM Bypass. All transfers include inside-terminal name-board collection and operations controller oversight from aircraft block-off to accommodation arrival.

## PSARA licensed operators

Transfer operators hold West Bengal PSARA licences under the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act 2005. Licence confirmation is provided on request.
