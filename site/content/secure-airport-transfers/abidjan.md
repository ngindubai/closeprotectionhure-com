Felix Houphouet-Boigny International Airport sits at the edge of Abidjan's most challenging transit zone. The route from ABJ to the principal business districts of Le Plateau, Cocody, and Zone 4 crosses communes that FCDO and US OSAC reporting identify as carrying the highest vehicle crime rates in Abidjan. Unvetted airport taxis operating from the ABJ kerb are a documented vector for robbery of foreign arrivals.

## The ABJ arrival risk window

The period between clearing baggage claim and reaching a secure vehicle is the most exposed moment in any Abidjan itinerary. Inside-terminal collection by a Ministry of Interior-licensed driver eliminates the kerb exposure. An operations controller tracking the route from collection to destination addresses the transit risk through Yopougon and at the Ebrié Lagoon bridge chokepoints.

## What Abidjan secure transfers cover

An Abidjan secure airport transfer covers: inside-terminal ABJ collection; Ministry of Interior-licensed driver and vehicle; Yopougon/Abobo route management; operations controller monitoring throughout; Ebrié Lagoon bridge protocol; pre-advance hotel liaison; and the departure transfer on the same terms.
