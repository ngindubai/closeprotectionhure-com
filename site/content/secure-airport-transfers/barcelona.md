Barcelona El Prat Airport (BCN) lies approximately 12km southwest of the city, with a standard transfer of 30-40 minutes to Eixample via the Ronda Litoral. Mossos d'Esquadra crime data from 2025 documents specific pickpocketing activity in the BCN arrivals zone, informing the counter-surveillance protocol applied at collection. Transfer operators are licensed under Law 5/2014 on Private Security and regulated by the Ministerio del Interior.

## Spain regulatory framework

All Barcelona transfer operators hold current credentials under Law 5/2014 on Private Security (Ley de Seguridad Privada). The Ministerio del Interior (Ministry of Interior) provides licensing oversight and audits compliance across private security operators throughout Spain.
