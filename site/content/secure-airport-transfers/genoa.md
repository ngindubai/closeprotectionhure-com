Most transfer services treat these as interchangeable. They aren't. The airport run is a straightforward 15 to 20 minute drive via the A26, but the cruise terminal brings a documented problem the airport doesn't: unsolicited approaches from unlicensed transfer operators working the landside crowd on high-volume turnaround days. Our name-board collection protocol positions the driver at the point of exit before the principal arrives, precisely to remove that decision from the principal altogether.

The route itself carries a low risk profile in FCDO terms, with the main practical friction being port-area congestion during peak hours rather than any pattern of targeted crime.
