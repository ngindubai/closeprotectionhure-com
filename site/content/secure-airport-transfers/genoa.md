The real complication in Genoa sits at Stazione Marittima, the cruise terminal, where very high passenger volumes on embarkation and disembarkation days create exactly the crowded, disorientating conditions in which unlicensed transfer operators have been documented making unsolicited approaches to arriving passengers. Whether a client is landing at GOA or stepping off a ship at the terminal, the first few minutes on the ground are where a secure transfer earns its value, and that starts with the client knowing precisely who is meeting them before they arrive.

Beyond that first moment, the challenge is Genoa itself.
