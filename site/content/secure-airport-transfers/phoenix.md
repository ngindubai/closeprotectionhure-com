However, Phoenix's extreme summer climate (42-46C from June through September) and moderate vehicle crime on the I-10 corridor create distinct transfer risks that a pre-arranged, licensed service addresses directly.

The I-10 freeway and SR-51 (Piestewa Freeway) are the primary inbound routes from PHX. Peak congestion on the I-10 eastbound around the Deck Park Tunnel is consistent on weekday mornings. ADOT AZ 511 2024 traffic data informs route planning; AZ-202 (Red Mountain Freeway) provides an alternative for Scottsdale and Tempe movements. Summer heat protocols apply across all June-September transfers.
