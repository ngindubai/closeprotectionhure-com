Bangui M'Poko International Airport (BGF) is 5km from central Bangui, but the transfer environment cannot be assessed using standard commercial criteria. The FCDO advises against all travel to the Central African Republic as of 2026. The US State Department maintains a Level 4 Do Not Travel advisory, citing armed group activity including from CPC-affiliated factions operating throughout the country and in and around the capital. No commercial secure airport transfer service operates in Bangui on a routine basis.

## The BGF arrival environment

Bangui M'Poko International receives limited international services, primarily Air France connections via Paris and regional flights from Central and West African hubs. The airport sits within a security environment shaped by ongoing civil conflict, the presence of MINUSCA peacekeeping forces, and Africa Corps personnel contracted to the CAR government. The 5km transfer from BGF into Bangui requires coordinated security arrangements that go well beyond standard transfer protocols.
