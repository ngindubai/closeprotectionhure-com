Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD) is 22km from Hyderabad's HITEC City IT corridor via the Outer Ring Road, with consistent 25-35 minute transfer times outside peak periods. Hyderabad Traffic Police 2024 records the ORR as relatively free-flowing compared to inner-city routes, making it the primary transfer artery for the city's extensive corporate campus and pharmaceutical sector principal population.

## The Hyderabad airport transfer environment

The Outer Ring Road (ORR) connects HYD to HITEC City, the Financial District, and central Hyderabad (Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills) with predictable transfer benchmarks. Southwest monsoon rainfall (June-September) requires India Meteorological Department data integration into route planning, particularly at the Gachibowli and Raidurgam ORR interchanges.

## PSARA Telangana licensed operators

Transfer operators hold Telangana PSARA licences under the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act 2005. Licence confirmation is provided on request.
