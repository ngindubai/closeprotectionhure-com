Utrecht has no airport of its own, and visiting principals typically arrive via Amsterdam Schiphol, either by road in 45 to 60 minutes or by a direct train to Utrecht Centraal in around 30 minutes, or via Eindhoven Airport, roughly 40 minutes away by road. Utrecht Centraal is the Netherlands' busiest railway station, handling approximately 110,000 passengers a day according to ProRail data, and its integration with the Hoog Catharijne shopping complex creates a dense public environment where pickpocketing is a documented risk. A fixed-point name-board collection inside the station concourse manages this handover without requiring the principal to navigate the complex independently.

A substantial proportion of Utrecht bookings serve corporate visitors to Rabobank's Papendorp headquarters, a.s.r.'s head office, or events at the Jaarbeurs Utrecht conference centre, and these are typically routed directly from the airport or station rather than via a city-centre hotel first.
