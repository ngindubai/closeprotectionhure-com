Adelaide Airport (ADL) is 7km from the CBD, offering one of Australia's shortest capital-city airport transfer distances. Transfer times of 15-20 minutes outside peak periods and the single-terminal layout make ADL consistently the most straightforward major Australian airport for principal-level secure transfers, with peak-hour delays typically adding only 10-15 minutes.

## The Adelaide airport transfer environment

Sir Donald Bradman Drive and the A1 Expressway provide the primary corridor from ADL to Adelaide CBD. Adelaide Oval event periods (AFL, international cricket) can affect King William Street approach routes; operations team pre-planning covers these event windows. Regional transfers to Barossa Valley and McLaren Vale are available on the same basis.

## Consumer and Business Services SA licensed operators

Transfer operators hold current CBS licences under the Security Industry Act 2007 (SA). Licence status is verifiable through the CBS South Australia public register.
