## The Wellington airport transfer environment

SH-1 Cobham Drive and the Wellington Urban Motorway form the primary route from WLG to the CBD. The Terrace Tunnel is the main congestion point during peak hours; the Mount Victoria Tunnel and Oriental Parade provide an alternative for CBD and southern suburb destinations. Wind monitoring is integrated into flight-tracking to manage transfer timing for WLG arrivals.

All secure transfer operators in New Zealand must hold licences under the Private Security Personnel and Private Investigators Act 2010, administered by MBIE.
