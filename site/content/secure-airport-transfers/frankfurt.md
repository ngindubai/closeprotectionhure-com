Frankfurt Airport Fraport (FRA) lies approximately 12km southwest of the city centre, with a standard transfer of 15-30 minutes to the Bankenviertel via the A3. With over 60 million passengers per year (Fraport AG, 2024), FRA's terminal volume and recurrent media presence shape the counter-surveillance protocol applied at collection for high-profile arrivals. Transfer operators are licensed under Section 34a of the Gewerbeordnung and regulated by the HLKA.

## Germany regulatory framework

All Frankfurt transfer operators hold current credentials under Section 34a of the Trade Regulation Act (Gewerbeordnung). The Hessian State Criminal Police Office (HLKA - Hessisches Landeskriminalamt) provides licensing oversight and audits compliance across private security operators in Hesse.
