The Hanoi urban road environment features very high motorcycle density in the Old Quarter and Hoan Kiem district; Vietnam Ministry of Public Security 2024 road safety data records elevated road fatality rates at a national level. ## The Hanoi airport transfer environment

The Vo Nguyen Giap expressway is the fastest inbound route under normal conditions. Peak congestion concentrates at the Hanoi city approach end of Nhat Tan Bridge during the 07:30-09:00 and 17:00-19:30 peaks. The Old Quarter's narrow streets require a driver with local knowledge of approach and egress routes. Ongoing construction on Hanoi's ring roads (Vanh Dai 2 and Vanh Dai 3) is tracked in route planning.

## Licensing and verification in Hanoi

Secure transfer operators in Vietnam must hold a valid Ministry of Public Security business licence under Decree 96/2016/ND-CP. The licence status of any operator is verified before engagement.
