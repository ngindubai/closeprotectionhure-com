Noi Bai International Airport (HAN) is 35km from central Hanoi and all ground transfers rely on the NH1 and Nhat Tan Bridge road corridor, a 40-60 minute journey outside peak periods. Hanoi City Police 2024 identifies the kerbside taxi zone at Noi Bai as a consistent point of unlicensed transport operations targeting international arrivals, making pre-arranged, permit-holding operator collection the appropriate baseline for principal-level transfers.

## The Hanoi airport transfer environment

The NH1, Nhat Tan Bridge, and Ring Road 3 form the principal corridors from HAN to the Hoan Kiem, Ba Dinh, and West Lake business areas. Hanoi Traffic Management Centre real-time data is incorporated into all transfer route planning. Tet holiday periods generate extreme congestion requiring extended transfer windows.

## Ministry of Public Security permit operators

Transfer operators hold Ministry of Public Security permits under Decree 96/2016/ND-CP. Permit confirmation is provided on request.
