Leon M'ba International Airport (LBV) is 12km from Quartier Louis and the central Libreville business district via Boulevard Triomphal Omar Bongo. The transfer corridor carries elevated robbery risk after dark, a pattern documented in FCDO Gabon travel advice (2026). Following the August 2023 military coup, military checkpoints remain active on key transit routes, making pre-arranged Interior Ministry licensed collection the required approach for professional arrivals into the Gabonese capital.

## The LBV arrival environment

Leon M'ba International serves all international flights into Libreville, including Air France connections from Paris and regional services via Addis Ababa, Casablanca, and Nairobi. The airport's arrivals area is managed but the kerb and immediate approach roads carry informal transport pressure. The 12km transfer to Quartier Louis and the oil sector office districts is the primary security exposure for arriving professionals.
