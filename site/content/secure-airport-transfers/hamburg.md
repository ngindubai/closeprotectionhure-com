Hamburg Airport Helmut Schmidt (HAM) lies approximately 12km northwest of the city centre, with a standard transfer of 25-30 minutes to HafenCity and the Innenstadt via the A7. Transfer operators are licensed under Section 34a of the Gewerbeordnung and regulated by the LKA Hamburg, providing a verifiable credential framework for clients arranging secure transfers in the city.

## Germany regulatory framework

All Hamburg transfer operators hold current credentials under Section 34a of the Trade Regulation Act (Gewerbeordnung). The Hamburg Criminal Police Office (LKA Hamburg - Landeskriminalamt Hamburg) provides licensing oversight and audits compliance across private security operators in the city-state of Hamburg.
