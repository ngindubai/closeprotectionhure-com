WA Police Force Crime Statistics 2024 and resources-sector intelligence assessments identify high-value corporate principals in the mining and energy sector as a documented targeting category in Perth. ## The Perth airport transfer environment

Perth's geographic isolation from Australia's east coast means that the resources sector drives a concentrated pattern of executive travel through PER, creating predictable movement profiles that benefit from active counter-surveillance and route variation. Great Eastern Highway and Tonkin Highway both serve as transfer corridors, and route selection is made based on the destination, time of travel, and current intelligence. Inter-terminal transfers between the PER international terminal and the domestic cluster are coordinated as part of the same transfer plan for fly-in/fly-out principals.

## Licensing and verification in Perth

All drivers providing secure transfers in Perth hold a current licence under the Western Australia Security and Related Activities (Control) Act 1996, administered by the WA Police Force Licensing Enforcement Division. This covers both individual security personnel and the companies employing them. Licence status is verified before any driver is engaged.
