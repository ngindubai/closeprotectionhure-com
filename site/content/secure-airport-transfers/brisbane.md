Australia's generally low crime environment means the primary risk at BNE arrivals is not violent crime but the unvetted transport contact documented by QPS Crime Statistics 2024 at international taxi ranks, where unlicensed operators target arriving passengers unfamiliar with local conventions. ## The Brisbane airport transfer environment

The Gateway Motorway (M1) is the standard corridor between BNE and the Brisbane CBD, with the Airport Link tunnel providing an alternative that avoids surface congestion on the northern arterials. International arrivals at BNE pass through Australian Border Force, customs, and biosecurity screening, which extends the post-landing window and requires planning flexibility in the collection timing. All transfer plans account for this additional processing time.

## Licensing and verification in Brisbane

All drivers providing secure transfers in Brisbane hold a current licence under the Queensland Security Providers Act 1993. This legislation governs both individual security personnel and the companies they work for, requiring background checks and ongoing compliance. Licence status is verified against the QPS register before any driver is engaged.
