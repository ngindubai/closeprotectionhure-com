## The GBE arrival environment

Gaborone serves international arrivals from Johannesburg, Nairobi, Addis Ababa, and London. The professional client base spans the diamond sector (Debswana, rough diamond trading), SADC institutional visitors, development finance organisations, and regional corporate executives. Cross-border road arrivals from Johannesburg via Tlokweng Gate are a common alternative to direct GBE arrivals.
