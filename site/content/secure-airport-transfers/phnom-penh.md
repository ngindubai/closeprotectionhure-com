Phnom Penh International Airport is the primary gateway for international arrivals in Cambodia, serving corporate visitors, NGO and UN agency staff, diplomatic travellers, and the country's growing tourism and hospitality economy. The airport transfer in Phnom Penh addresses a moderate risk environment: the principal security considerations are informal transport scams and opportunistic crime at the arrivals zone rather than organised armed threat. Pre-arranged Ministry of Interior-registered collection inside the terminal and operations controller tracking convert this from a tourism-grade transport arrangement into a professionally managed security operation.

## The PNH arrival environment

Phnom Penh International Airport serves all international and domestic flights into the capital. The FCDO advises normal travel precautions for Cambodia, noting petty crime including drive-by theft as the primary concern. The PNH arrivals zone has informal transport operators whose use is associated with higher incident rates than pre-booked vetted transport. Source: FCDO Cambodia travel advice (2026).
