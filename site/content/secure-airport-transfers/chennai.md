Chennai International Airport (MAA) is 16km from the central business district and 12km from the OMR IT corridor, with transfer times of 30-45 minutes outside peak hours on the GST Road and Inner Ring Road. Tamil Nadu Government Emergency Management 2024 records the northeast monsoon (October-December) as a significant transfer-planning consideration, with the GST Road and Kathipara underpass historically affected by surface flooding.

## The Chennai airport transfer environment

The GST Road (NH44), Inner Ring Road, and Rajiv Gandhi Salai form the principal corridors from MAA to CBD, OMR IT parks, and south Chennai destinations. Chennai City Traffic Police 2024 records significant peak congestion at the Kathipara Junction. All transfers include inside-terminal collection and operations controller oversight from aircraft block-off to accommodation arrival.

## PSARA Tamil Nadu licensed operators

Transfer operators hold Tamil Nadu PSARA licences under the Private Security Agencies Regulation Act 2005. Licence confirmation is provided on request.
