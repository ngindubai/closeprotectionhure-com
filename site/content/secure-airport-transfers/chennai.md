Chennai International Airport handles both domestic and international arrivals for Tamil Nadu's capital city. The 16km GST Road corridor from MAA to Anna Salai is subject to significant peak-hour congestion and, critically, to northeast monsoon flooding from October to December that can disrupt the primary NH-48 route. PSARA-licensed drivers, monsoon-season alternative routing, and operations controller oversight from arrival to accommodation address both the standard congestion and the seasonal flood risk.

## The Chennai airport transfer environment

GST Road (NH-48) is the primary inbound route from MAA. Peak congestion at 08:00-10:00 and 17:30-20:00 and monsoon-related road flooding from October to December are the two primary planning variables. Chennai Traffic Police 2024 data informs route selection; Mount-Poonamallee Road and Sardar Patel Road alternatives are pre-mapped for flood conditions.

## Licensing and verification in Chennai

Secure transfer operators in Tamil Nadu must hold a valid PSARA 2005 licence from the Tamil Nadu SIA. The licence status is verified before engagement.
