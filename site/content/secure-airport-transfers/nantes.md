Nantes combines a working regional airport at NTE, a historic old town around the Bouffay quarter, and a position as the gateway to the Loire Valley chateau circuit further east, which together produce a varied client base ranging from corporate visitors to those extending their trip into wine country. FCDO France travel advice (2024) documents vehicle crime at motorway service areas along the N844 and NTE corridor as a pattern primarily affecting unattended vehicles, alongside pickpocketing at Gare de Nantes and in the Bouffay quarter. A pre-arranged transfer with name-board collection avoids the exposure both patterns rely on.
