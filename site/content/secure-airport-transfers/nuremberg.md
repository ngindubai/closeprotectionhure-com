Nuremberg Airport (NUE) sits approximately 5km north of the city centre, a straightforward transfer under normal conditions but one that changes character entirely during the city's major trade fair weeks. Spielwarenmesse and BrauBeviale each bring exceptional hotel and transfer demand across Nuremberg, and pre-booked vetted transport is the practical response rather than an optional precaution during those periods.

The transfer service also covers the pre-surveyed route to NurnbergMesse itself, one of the world's largest exhibition venues, and the wider corridor to Herzogenaurach for executives whose itinerary includes Adidas or Puma headquarters business.
