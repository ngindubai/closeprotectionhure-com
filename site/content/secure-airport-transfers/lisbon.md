Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport (LIS) lies approximately 7km north of Baixa, with a standard drive of 20-30 minutes to the city centre via the IC2 corridor. Transfer operators in Portugal are licensed under Law 34/2013 and regulated by the PSP (Policia de Seguranca Publica), providing a defined credential framework clients can verify before travel.

## Portugal regulatory framework

All Lisbon transfer operators hold current credentials under Law 34/2013 (Lei de Seguranca Privada). The PSP (Policia de Seguranca Publica) provides licensing oversight and is responsible for auditing compliance across private security operators in Portugal.
