Secure transfer planning in Bordeaux has to treat those as genuinely separate problems. The city transfer is a straightforward 20 to 25 minute run via the rocade under normal conditions; the estate transfer covers rural D-roads with longer, less predictable journey times, patchy mobile signal in parts of the Medoc peninsula, and a longer practical distance to the nearest emergency response point than anywhere in central [Bordeaux](/cities/bordeaux/).
