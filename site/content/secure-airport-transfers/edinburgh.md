Edinburgh Airport (EDI) lies approximately 8km west of the city centre, with a standard transfer of 20-30 minutes to New Town and Lothian Road via the A8. The Edinburgh Trams route, which runs along the primary airport corridor, and Edinburgh Festival in August both introduce specific route-planning requirements that are built into the operations controller's monitoring brief. Transfer operators are licensed by the Security Industry Authority (SIA) under the Private Security Industry Act 2001, with licence status independently verifiable.

## UK regulatory framework

All Edinburgh transfer operators hold a current licence from the Security Industry Authority (SIA) under the Private Security Industry Act 2001. The SIA is the statutory regulator for the private security industry across the United Kingdom, and licence status can be verified via the SIA public licence checker.
