Malaga functions as the principal air gateway for both the city itself and the wider Costa del Sol, with Malaga-Costa del Sol Airport handling arrivals bound for Marbella, Puerto Banus, and Estepona as well as central Malaga's historic centre and port district. FCDO Spain travel advice (2024) documents hire-car theft at AGP collection points and at service stations along the A-7 corridor as a recurring pattern, primarily affecting self-drive visitors rather than pre-arranged transfers with a vetted driver. Pickpocketing around Calle Larios in the central shopping district is a separate, lower-severity risk documented in the same advice.

The 8-kilometre run from AGP into central Malaga is straightforward, but clients continuing west along the A-7 to the Costa del Sol resort towns should plan for a further 45 to 60 minutes depending on the exact destination and time of year, since summer peak season adds notable congestion.
