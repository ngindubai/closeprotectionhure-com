Kigali International Airport is approximately 12 kilometres from the city centre, making the Kigali airport transfer among East Africa's operationally most straightforward. Rwanda National Police-compliant vetted drivers with inside-terminal collection provide the appropriate professional baseline for diplomatic, conference, and corporate arrivals.

## The Kigali arrival standard

Kigali's comparatively low crime profile means the airport transfer risk environment is less acute than Dakar or Kinshasa. The professional transfer standard is nonetheless the appropriate baseline for diplomatic delegations, senior executives, and conference visitors: inside-terminal collection eliminates the unescorted wait at the taxi zone, and operations controller tracking provides the accountability chain that professional organisations require.

## What secure transfers cover in Kigali

A Kigali secure airport transfer covers: inside-terminal collection at KGL; operations controller tracking; transfer to Kiyovu, Nyarutarama, Kimihurura, or Kigali's conference hotels; and the return departure transfer with adequate check-in lead time. For conference delegations, a multi-vehicle fleet transfer coordinated through a single operations controller is available.
