Lungi International Airport (FNA) is separated from Freetown by the Sierra Leone River estuary. The absence of a road bridge means that the Lungi crossing -- helicopter, ferry, or overland -- is the defining operational planning task for every Freetown airport transfer. FCDO Sierra Leone travel advice (2026) advises a high degree of caution, and the US State Department rates Sierra Leone at Level 2.

## The FNA arrival environment

Night arrivals require helicopter crossing or enhanced ferry protocols.
