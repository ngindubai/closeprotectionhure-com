## The Kuala Lumpur transfer environment

The primary transfer challenge in Kuala Lumpur is not airport security but the distance and congestion between KLIA/KLIA2 and the city. Subang Skypark's central location makes it the preferred option for private aviation principals. Terminal confusion between KLIA Main and KLIA2 is a significant practical risk that pre-confirmation eliminates.

## KLIA, KLIA2, and Subang logistics
