Tunis-Carthage International Airport is approximately 9 kilometres from Les Berges du Lac and 14 kilometres from Carthage. The short distance makes the Tunis airport transfer operationally straightforward, but pre-booked Ministry of Interior-licensed collection and operations controller oversight provide the professional baseline for diplomatic, conference, and corporate arrivals.

## The Tunis-Carthage transfer standard

Tunisia's medium-risk profile and the comparatively short airport distance mean the Tunis transfer risk environment is less acute than Dakar or Lima. The professional transfer model provides: structured arrival through inside-terminal collection rather than the unregulated taxi zone; identity-verified vetted driver; and operations controller accountability. For delegates to the Kigali Convention Centre equivalent, this is the standard their organisations require.

## What secure transfers cover in Tunis

A Tunis secure airport transfer covers: inside-terminal collection at TUN; operations controller tracking; transfer to Les Berges du Lac, Carthage, or La Marsa accommodation; and the return departure transfer with adequate check-in lead time.
