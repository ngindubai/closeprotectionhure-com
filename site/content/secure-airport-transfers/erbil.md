## The EBL arrival environment

Erbil International Airport serves all international flights into the Kurdistan Region. It has been targeted by Iran-aligned militia rocket and drone attacks, with the US Consulate and coalition military facilities in and around Erbil also targeted. The FCDO advises against all but essential travel to the KRI and recommends registering with the British Embassy before arrival. Operations controller tracking begins from the moment the principal's flight lands, not from terminal exit. Source: FCDO Iraq travel advice (2026); US State Department Iraq advisory (2026).
