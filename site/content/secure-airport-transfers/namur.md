Namur has no airport of its own, so every secure transfer starts at either Brussels Airport or Brussels South Charleroi Airport (BSCA), followed by a direct IC rail connection to Brussels Central of roughly an hour, or a comparable road transfer matched to the principal's international routing.

The transfer service's distinguishing feature is government-quarter access planning: visits to the Parliament of Wallonia or the Elysette are coordinated with the host institution in advance, particularly during sitting weeks when access arrangements can change on short notice.
