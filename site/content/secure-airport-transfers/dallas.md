## The Dallas airport transfer environment

The I-635 (LBJ Freeway) and I-35E serve as the primary arteries between DFW and Downtown Dallas. Both corridors experience significant congestion during morning and evening peak periods, and carjacking awareness protocols apply throughout these routes. For principals arriving at Dallas Love Field, the 11km route via Stemmons Freeway presents its own congestion patterns and requires a pre-surveyed alternative. All transfer routes are planned against current incident data drawn from DPD Crime Report 2024.

## Licensing and verification in Dallas

All drivers providing secure transfers in Dallas hold a current licence under Texas DPS Chapter 1702, administered by the Texas Department of Public Safety. The public licence register is verified before any driver is engaged. This licensing requirement covers both private security operations and commercial transportation with security functions, providing a baseline of vetted, accountable personnel for every transfer.
