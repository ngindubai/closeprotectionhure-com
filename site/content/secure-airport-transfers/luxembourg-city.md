Luxembourg Airport Findel (LUX) lies approximately 6km east of the city centre, with a standard transfer of 15-20 minutes via route de Treves - one of the shortest airport-to-centre distances of any European capital. The regular transit of EU institution principals and diplomatic personnel through LUX shapes the collection protocol and counter-surveillance approach applied as standard. Transfer operators are licensed under the Law of 12 November 2002 on private security and regulated by the Grand Ducal Police.

## Luxembourg regulatory framework

All Luxembourg City transfer operators hold current credentials under the Law of 12 November 2002 on private security. The Grand Ducal Police (Police Grand-Ducale) provides licensing oversight and audits compliance across private security operators in Luxembourg.
