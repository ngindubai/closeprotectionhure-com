## The Shenzhen transfer environment

The primary concern for executive arrivals at SZX is not street crime but business intelligence exposure and compliance with the specific legal framework that governs security operations in China. The Ministry of Public Security Order No. 564 (2010) governs all commercial security activity. Any protective arrangement that does not account for these constraints creates legal risk for the principal rather than reducing operational risk.
