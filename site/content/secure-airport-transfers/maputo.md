Maputo International Airport is approximately 6 kilometres from Polana Cimento and 8 kilometres from Sommerschield, making it among southern Africa's closer city-airport combinations. Despite the short distance, pre-booked Ministry of Interior-licensed collection and operations controller tracking provide the professional baseline that energy sector and corporate organisations require for their personnel arriving in Mozambique.

## The Maputo arrival window

Armed robbery and vehicle crime are documented across Maputo, including on the airport approach road and in the surrounding Mavalane area. Inside-terminal collection eliminates the unescorted kerb wait that creates the primary arrival vulnerability. Night-arrival protocol adjusts the transfer management for late-flight arrivals when the Maputo risk profile is elevated.

## What secure transfers cover in Maputo

A Maputo secure airport transfer covers: inside-terminal collection at MPM; operations controller tracking; armed robbery counter-protocol routing to Sommerschield, Polana Cimento, or Bairro Central accommodation; night-arrival protocol; and the return departure transfer with adequate check-in lead time.
