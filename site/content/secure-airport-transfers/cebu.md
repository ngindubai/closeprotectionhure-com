Cebu is the Philippines' second city and the commercial centre of the Visayas region, with a growing business process outsourcing (BPO), manufacturing, and tourism economy. Mactan-Cebu International Airport serves the city from Mactan Island, connected to Cebu City by two bridges. The bridge dependency creates a specific transfer variable absent in continental airports, and the Philippine typhoon belt position makes seasonal disruption planning a normal part of transfer management.

## The CEB Mactan transfer context

The primary transfer variables in Cebu are the Marcelo Fernan Bridge congestion and access dependency, and typhoon season disruption risk. PNP-PSG compliance under Republic Act 5487 is the baseline check for private security transport operators in the Philippines.

## What secure transfers cover

A Cebu secure airport transfer covers: inside-hall collection at CEB by a PNP-registered driver; operations controller tracking from CEB to destination; Marcelo Fernan Bridge real-time monitoring; typhoon season contingency planning; and return transfers from accommodation to CEB on the same terms.
