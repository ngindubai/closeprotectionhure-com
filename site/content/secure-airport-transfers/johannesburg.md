The Johannesburg airport transfer is the moment in any business trip to the city when the combination of visible luggage, unfamiliar surroundings, and the transit from a secured terminal to open-road vehicle movement creates the highest concentrated risk. OR Tambo International is South Africa's busiest airport and a well-documented location for approaches to foreign visitors. The carjacking statistics for the N3 corridor to Sandton are among the highest for any airport approach road in sub-Saharan Africa.

## The OR Tambo arrival risk environment

South African Police Service crime statistics and Gauteng security industry incident reporting consistently identify the OR Tambo approach corridor and the airport kerb zone as elevated-risk environments for arriving foreign visitors. Unvetted taxis from the kerb are the most common vector for robbery of airport arrivals. The pre-arranged, inside-terminal collection model eliminates the kerb exposure entirely.

## What secure transfers cover

A Johannesburg secure airport transfer covers: inside-terminal collection by a PSIRA-licensed driver; operations controller tracking throughout; carjacking counter-protocol routing; vehicle appropriate to the threat assessment; hotel security liaison before arrival; and the return departure transfer on the same terms. The transfer is the first and last security decision on any Johannesburg trip, and it sets the operational tone for everything in between.
