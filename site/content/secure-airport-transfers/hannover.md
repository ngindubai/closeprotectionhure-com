Hannover Messe sets the pace for most of the transfer work here. More than 4,000 exhibiting companies from over 70 countries attend a typical edition, an event widely regarded within manufacturing circles as the world's largest industrial trade fair, and drivers collecting principals at HAJ, roughly 11km north of the centre in Langenhagen, build extra time into fair-week transfers as a matter of course.

Continental AG's new global headquarters campus, Continental-Plaza, opened here in December 2023, and drivers confirm access arrangements before any drop-off rather than treating a major corporate site like a hotel entrance. The Hauptbahnhof is worth flagging too: Bundespolizei data reported 1,606 offences there between January and May 2025, and while a joint policing and city security initiative running October 2024 to September 2025 cut violent crime by more than 20 percent, drivers still favour a direct road transfer over routing through the station on foot.

For itineraries that extend to Wolfsburg, home to Volkswagen Group's headquarters and just 30 minutes away by direct ICE train, the same driver can cover both legs without a handover. Drivers hold German professional licences and, where the job carries a protective element, Sachkundeprufung certification under the Bewachungsverordnung.
