Hannover Airport is close enough, about 11km north in Langenhagen, that the transfer logistics rarely surprise anyone, except during one week a year. Hannover Messe, widely described within industrial circles as the world's largest industrial trade fair, draws over 4,000 exhibiting companies from more than 70 countries, and fair week brings exceptional hotel and transfer demand across the entire city. Booking well ahead of April's show dates is the single most useful thing a visiting executive can do.

Drivers track the inbound flight and are positioned at the terminal before the principal clears customs, using a clearly printed name board for collection rather than an open approach, sensible given that Bundespolizei-sourced reporting flagged the Hauptbahnhof among Germany's higher-incident stations, even as a joint police and city initiative running through September 2025 delivered a documented, more than 20 percent cut in violent crime there. Vehicles are maintained executive saloons or MPVs, and where the transfer includes a protective dimension, the driver holds current IHK Sachkundeprufung certification.

For visits that combine Hannover Messe with Volkswagen Group business, the same driver covers the roughly 90km corridor to Wolfsburg, alongside the roughly 30-minute direct ICE rail alternative, as a routine extension. Continental AG's Continental-Plaza headquarters, opened December 2023, is a short, straightforward run from HAJ.
