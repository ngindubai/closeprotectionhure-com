Monterrey is Mexico's primary industrial and manufacturing city, the home of major Mexican conglomerates in steel, cement, beverages, and logistics, and a significant location for North American nearshoring investment. The 24-kilometre MTY airport-to-city corridor passes through environments that carry carjacking and express crime risk that is materially higher than in most comparable Latin American commercial centres. For business visitors, the airport transfer window is among the highest-risk moments in a Monterrey trip.

## The MTY transfer risk environment

Nuevo Leon State police statistics and private security incident data consistently identify the MTY airport approach roads and the Periferico corridor as elevated-risk zones for arriving business travellers. The risk is not diffuse: it concentrates in the kerb and car park zones immediately outside arrivals, and on specific road sections during specific time windows.

## What secure transfers cover

A Monterrey secure airport transfer covers: inside-hall collection at MTY by a CNSP-registered driver; pre-shared vehicle and driver details for verification before boarding; operations controller tracking throughout; carjacking counter-protocol routing; and return transfers from accommodation to MTY on the same terms.
