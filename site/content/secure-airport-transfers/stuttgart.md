Stuttgart Airport's position, roughly 12km south of the centre and directly beside Messe Stuttgart, makes it one of the more convenient German airports for combined transfer planning during a trade-fair week. The rest of the year, the more common destination is one of the two automotive giants headquartered here: Mercedes-Benz Group AG in Untertuerkheim or Porsche AG in Zuffenhausen, both of which run formal site accreditation that a transfer booking needs to account for in advance rather than treating the plant gate like a hotel entrance.

Drivers hold German professional licences and, where the job includes a protective element, IHK Region Stuttgart qualification under the Bewachungsverordnung. FCDO Germany's 2026 guidance places crime broadly in line with the UK, so the standard nationwide concern, pickpocketing at transport hubs, is the main practical consideration at STR, addressed with the usual printed name board and identity confirmation before luggage changes hands. During AMB, Blechexpo, or other major Messe Stuttgart exhibitions, drivers build extra time into the run given the congestion that builds on approach roads.
