## Germany regulatory framework

All Munich transfer operators hold current credentials under Section 34a of the Trade Regulation Act (Gewerbeordnung). The Bavarian State Criminal Police Office (BLKA) provides licensing oversight and audits compliance across private security operators in Bavaria.
