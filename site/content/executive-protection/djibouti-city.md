Executive protection in Djibouti City is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against regional maritime instability and border-area risk: advance work on venues and routes, a written risk assessment, an operations controller, and licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

A Djibouti City EP programme covers pre-advance of the JIB transfer and all confirmed venues, low-profile principal management in the Plateau du Serpent and port districts, military facility coordination, Hub EP staging for onward Horn of Africa travel, and crisis response via Djibouti-Ambouli Airport.
