The city's 2018 Christmas Market attack, which killed five people and wounded eleven before a 700-officer manhunt ended with the gunman's death, remains a genuine part of that picture rather than historical footnote, and France's Vigipirate Urgence Attentat posture has applied nationally since.

CNAPS-authorised CPOs work unarmed as standard and coordinate directly with institutional venue security when protecting delegates or officials on Parliament or Council of Europe business, rather than operating independently inside those perimeters. Neuhof and Hautepierre, peripheral districts with documented drug dealing, sit outside any legitimate route, while Neustadt and Orangerie serve as the preferred accommodation district for visiting executives.
