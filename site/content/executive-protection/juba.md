Executive protection in Juba is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies when it owes a duty of care to an executive facing post-conflict instability and intercommunal and political violence: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and 2021 Act licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management.

A standard Juba EP programme uses a minimum two-person team, UNMISS coordination as a baseline, armoured vehicles for elevated-risk principals, strict after-dark movement protocols, and medevac planning via Nairobi or Kampala.
