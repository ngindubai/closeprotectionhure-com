Executive protection in Cape Town is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies when it owes a duty of care to an executive facing carjacking, follow-home robbery, and kidnapping: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and Grade A PSIRA-licensed armed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management.

PSIRA Grade A registration is the legal and quality baseline. The South African private security industry is large - one of the largest per capita in the world - and highly variable in quality. Regulatory compliance is a necessary but not sufficient filter; specific operational experience of Cape Town's geography, crime patterns, and the specific risks of the Atlantic Seaboard, CBD, and V and A Waterfront environments is what distinguishes effective EP from compliance-box-ticking in this market.
