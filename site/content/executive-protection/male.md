Executive protection in Male is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against gang violence and knife crime alongside political instability: advance work on venues and routes, a written risk assessment, an operations controller, and Ministry of Home Affairs regulated secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management.

Ministry of Home Affairs-licensed close protection officers manage the MLE airport arrival, the 5-minute ferry or speedboat crossing to Male or Hulhumale, and ground escort across the compact Male city road network. For principals continuing to outer-atoll luxury resorts, seaplane and speedboat transfer coordination from MLE to properties including COMO Cocoa Island and Bandos Maldives is managed in advance. Outer-atoll resort security coordination is conducted with each property's own security team.
