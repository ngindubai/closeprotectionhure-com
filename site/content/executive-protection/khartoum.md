This page covers what a duty-of-care programme actually looks like here: remote risk assessment, continuous situation monitoring, and a documented decision framework for whether any Sudan-linked travel is undertaken at all. Where interests persist, planning is built around Port Sudan and confirmed evacuation arrangements, not a standard itinerary.
