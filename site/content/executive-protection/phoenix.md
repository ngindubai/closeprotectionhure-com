## Licensing and due diligence in Phoenix

All close protection officers deployed in Phoenix must be licensed under Arizona Revised Statutes Title 32, Chapter 26, administered by Arizona DPS Licensing Unit. Confirming individual CPO licensing before engagement is standard practice. The relevant authority maintains the register; confirming compliance before engagement is the non-negotiable due-diligence step.
