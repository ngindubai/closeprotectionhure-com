Executive protection in Maseru is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against armed robbery, street crime, and political instability: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and nationally licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

A Maseru EP programme covers road or air arrival management, movement security in the Kingsway and diplomatic areas, security for Ministry meetings, and medevac planning via Bloemfontein or Johannesburg.
