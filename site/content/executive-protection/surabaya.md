Surabaya is Indonesia's 2nd largest city and a major industrial and port hub. FCDO Indonesia 2026 rates Java at Level 1, making the city broadly accessible for corporate operations. Road traffic safety (NTMC Indonesia 2024: high road fatality rate) is the primary daily operational risk, and executive protection in Surabaya is centred on road safety advance planning, defensive driving protocols, and Polri BUJP-permitted operator deployment.

## Operating in Surabaya

The Ciputra World corridor and Wiyung/Pakuwon districts provide the primary EP base for HNWI and corporate principals. Juanda Airport transfers use the JIUTS toll road. Protest monitoring applies to all CBD and port-area movements. Road safety advance planning is integrated into every aspect of the EP assignment.
