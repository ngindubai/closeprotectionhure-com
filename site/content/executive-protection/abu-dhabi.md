It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive amid documented regional missile and drone activity and a strict legal environment: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and unarmed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

All EP companies and personnel must hold current MoI licences, and armed close protection requires specific MoI authorisation. ADGM on Al Maryah Island, the Corniche corporate district, and the ADNOC campus are the primary EP operational zones, each with distinct access control and advance work requirements that experienced Abu Dhabi CPOs understand.
