Executive protection in Accra is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against armed robbery and periodic political unrest: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

Our executive protection teams in Accra operate under the Private Security Organisations Act 2020, ensuring that all operators hold valid Ghana Police Service-overseen licences. The focus for most Accra engagements is on discreet, professionally conducted journey management and venue advance work that avoids disrupting the normal flow of a business itinerary while ensuring the principal is not exposed to avoidable risk. Airport transfers from Kotoka International, hotel advances in Cantonments and the Airport Residential Area, and guidance on after-dark movement in Osu are standard components of any Accra detail.
