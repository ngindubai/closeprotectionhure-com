Executive protection in Tirana is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against organised crime and rule-of-law and corruption-linked risk: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and DPSH-licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

A professional EP deployment for Tirana begins with advance work -- threat assessment, hotel and venue surveys, route pre-surveys, and medevac pre-arrangement to Vienna or Rome -- completed before the principal arrives. The protective posture during the visit covers: vetted transport for all movements from Tirana International Airport Mother Teresa (TIA), dedicated close protection for higher-risk engagements, residential security, and a documented emergency response plan. Post-deployment review produces a continuously improving security baseline for organisations with recurring Tirana travel.
