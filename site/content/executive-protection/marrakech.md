Marrakech is Morocco's primary destination for HNWI and high-value corporate travel. FCDO Morocco 2026 rates the city at Level 2 and OSAC Morocco 2024 records low violent crime overall, but the Medina and Djemaa el-Fna present specific petty crime and crowd management challenges that require professional EP advance work. DGSN-authorised executive protection covering Medina visits, Palmeraie stays, and Gueliz business operations provides the appropriate baseline for corporate and HNWI principals visiting Marrakech.

## Operating in Marrakech

Gueliz and Hivernage are the primary corporate and HNWI operating base. Palmeraie villa estates require approach road pre-survey and estate liaison. Medina visits require specific advance work as a distinct operation within the itinerary. All principal movements include operations controller tracking.
