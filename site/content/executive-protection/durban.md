Durban's eThekwini Metro records elevated carjacking, armed robbery, and vehicle crime across the city (SAPS Annual Crime Report 2023/24). PSIRA-registered executive protection with anti-carjacking vehicle protocols, advance venue work, and armed detail options is the appropriate baseline for corporate and HNWI principals visiting Durban. Umhlanga and La Lucia Ridge provide the lowest-risk operating base; CBD operations require specific advance planning.

## Operating in Durban

Umhlanga is the primary EP base for visiting HNWI and corporate principals, with direct N2 access to King Shaka Airport. La Lucia Ridge provides the main corporate office and meeting infrastructure. CBD operations require carjacking-aware advance work and timed movements. The N2 and M4 coastal route are both pre-surveyed for each assignment.

## Licensing and operator selection

All executive protection operators in Durban must hold PSIRA registration under PSIRA 2001. Armed officers additionally require Firearms Control Act 60/2000 competency.
