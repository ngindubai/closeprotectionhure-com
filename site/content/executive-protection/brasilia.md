Brasilia is Brazil's centre of government, diplomatic activity, and institutional decision-making. Executive protection in the capital requires a specific combination of low-profile professionalism for embassy and ministry environments, carjacking awareness for semi-urban transfer corridors, and DPF-registered operator credentials. FCDO Brazil 2026 rates the city at Level 2 and the Plano Piloto core presents a more manageable risk profile than Brazil's commercial cities, but peripheral zone exposure and semaforo carjacking risk mean professional EP remains the appropriate baseline for senior executives and HNWI visitors.

## Operating in Brasilia

The Plano Piloto core encompasses the government ministries, embassy quarter, and main corporate office towers. Lago Sul and Lago Norte provide the primary HNWI residential base for visiting principals. Advance work covers every venue and transfer leg; carjacking-aware routing is applied to all movements between the residential lakes and the Plano Piloto core.
