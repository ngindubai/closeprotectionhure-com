It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against terrorism awareness and protest disruption: advance work on venues and routes, a written risk assessment, an operations controller, and Sachkundeprufung-certified secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

Germany's Section 34a GewO regulatory framework, enforced through the IHK Sachkundeprufung examination, provides a strong legal baseline for commercial security work. The framework is consistently enforced and the regulated market is reliable. Beyond compliance, operational quality in Berlin is differentiated by specific experience of the government quarter's security infrastructure, the demonstration monitoring capability required for effective route management, and the cultural calibration - discreet, professional, unobtrusive - appropriate to Germany's corporate and diplomatic environment.
