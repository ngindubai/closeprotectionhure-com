Executive protection in Ouagadougou is not a deployable programme in the conventional sense; the highest FCDO and US State Department risk rating, active insurgency, and mass displacement rule out standard commercial deployment. This page covers what a duty-of-care approach actually requires: specialist Sahel conflict-environment operators, a documented decision framework for whether any Burkina Faso-linked travel proceeds at all, and case-by-case assessment for essential purposes only, not a standard itinerary.

For regional staging and operational management of Burkina Faso interests, [Abidjan](/cities/abidjan/) in Cote d'Ivoire is the primary hub. [Accra](/cities/accra/) provides the alternative regional base with direct OUA connections.
