The October 2015 railway station bombings, which killed 103 people at a peace rally 500 metres from the TCDD station, remain the defining mass-casualty precedent for Ankara EP briefings. The venue type targeted in that attack, a public gathering in a transport-adjacent public space, is directly relevant to route and venue risk scoring for any principal with a schedule near Ankara's government quarter.

Government ministry visits are the primary EP driver in Ankara. All major Turkish ministries, the TBMM, the Presidency, and the armed forces headquarters are concentrated in the Çankaya district, making the approach roads along Atatürk Bulvarı and into the government quarter a predictable and therefore surveilled route for high-value principals. EP teams vary approach routes and timing for every ministry visit and conduct advance surveys of parking and building access at each specific venue. EP vehicles are pre-positioned within the arrivals zone and the route is confirmed in advance.
