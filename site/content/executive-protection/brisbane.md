## Licensing and due diligence in Brisbane

All close protection officers deployed in Brisbane must be licensed under Security Providers Act 1993 (Queensland Police Service Weapons Licensing Branch). Confirming individual CPO licensing before engagement is standard practice. The relevant authority maintains the register; confirming compliance before engagement is the non-negotiable due-diligence step.
