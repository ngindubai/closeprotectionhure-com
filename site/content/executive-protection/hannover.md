Outside fair season, the picture is quieter and increasingly corporate. Wolfsburg, home to Volkswagen Group's global headquarters, sits about 90 kilometres away with a direct ICE train of roughly 30 minutes, so combined Hannover-Wolfsburg itineraries are common and worth planning as a single route rather than two separate trips.
