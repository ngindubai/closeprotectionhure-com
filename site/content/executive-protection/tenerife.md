Executive protection on Tenerife is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against tourist-area petty theft and mountain and coastal road risk: advance work on venues and routes, a written risk assessment, an operations controller, and SES-licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

The island's two-airport geography, Tenerife South (TFS) for resort arrivals and Tenerife North (TFN) for business travel, is the central planning variable for all Tenerife itineraries. SES-licensed officers, operating under the Ley de Seguridad Privada 5/2014, cover both arrival points and manage cross-island transfers between the Santa Cruz business district and the south coast hotel corridor. Venue advance at major resort properties and Santa Cruz conference facilities is standard for event deployments.
