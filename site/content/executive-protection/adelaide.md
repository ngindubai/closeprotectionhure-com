## Licensing and due diligence in Adelaide

All close protection officers deployed in Adelaide must be licensed under Security and Investigation Industry Act 1995 (SA), administered by Consumer and Business Services (CBS) SA. Confirming individual CPO licensing before engagement is standard practice. The relevant authority maintains the register; confirming compliance before engagement is the non-negotiable due-diligence step.
