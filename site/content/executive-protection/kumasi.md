FCDO Ghana 2026 rates the city at Level 2, with Ghana Police Service 2024 recording elevated market-area petty crime and NRSA Ghana 2024 identifying a high road fatality rate nationally. GPS-registered executive protection is the appropriate professional standard for senior principals operating in Kumasi, particularly for Kejetia Market visits, N6 road transfers, and Manhyia Palace-associated engagements.

## Operating in Kumasi

KNUST and Asokwa are the recommended base areas for corporate principals, with lower crime exposure and more manageable road access than Adum CBD and the Kejetia junction. Kejetia Market and Manhyia Palace visits require specific advance work and formation planning. N6 road transfers to Accra require road safety discipline protocols aligned with NRSA Ghana 2024 guidance.
