Executive protection in Chittagong is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against terrorism awareness and political instability and strike action: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and Ministry of Home Affairs registered secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management.

## The Chittagong EP environment

EP in Chittagong provides a structured security overlay for the principal movement cycle, calibrated to the current threat assessment covering terrorism, political strikes (hartals), and petty crime targeting port and garment-sector visitors. Source: FCDO Bangladesh travel advice (2026).

## What executive protection covers in Chittagong

An EP programme in Chittagong covers the full principal movement cycle: advance work at all venues, close escort during movements, accommodation security liaison, daily route variation, a documented emergency action plan, and medevac provider confirmation before arrival.
