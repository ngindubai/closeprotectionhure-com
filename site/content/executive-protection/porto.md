## Licensing and due diligence in Porto

All close protection officers deployed in Porto must be licensed under Law No. 34/2013 on private security activities (PSP Portugal). Confirming individual CPO licensing before engagement is standard practice. The relevant authority maintains the register; confirming compliance before engagement is the non-negotiable due-diligence step.
