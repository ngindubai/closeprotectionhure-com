Executive protection in Skopje is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against organised crime and historical ethnic-tension exposure: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and Ministry of Interior authorised secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management.

A professional EP deployment for Skopje begins with advance work -- threat assessment, hotel and venue surveys, route pre-surveys, and medevac pre-arrangement to Belgrade or Vienna -- completed before the principal arrives. The protective posture during the visit covers: vetted transport for all movements from Skopje Alexander the Great Airport (SKP), dedicated close protection for higher-risk engagements, residential security, and a documented emergency response plan. Post-deployment review produces a continuously improving security baseline for organisations with recurring Skopje travel.
