Thessaloniki is northern Greece's commercial capital and the host city of the Thessaloniki International Fair, one of southeast Europe's most significant annual trade events. The city draws corporate principals from Greek and international businesses across the Balkans and eastern Mediterranean. The security environment is low-moderate: the FCDO Greece travel advice (2024) records occasional civil unrest and protest activity in central districts, with TIF season bringing substantially increased crowd density and associated risk to the city.

Hellenic Police-licensed close protection officers manage principal movement across Aristotle Square, the waterfront corridor, TIF grounds, and the Hyatt Regency Thessaloniki. Venue advance at the Helexpo TIF complex is a specific competency for September deployments. Thessaloniki Makedonia Airport (SKG), 13km from the city, is managed with inside-terminal collection and operations-controller tracking on all arrivals and departures.
