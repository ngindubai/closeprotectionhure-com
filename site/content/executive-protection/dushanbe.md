It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against terrorism, regional spillover, and political repression: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and Ministry of Internal Affairs licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management.

## The Dushanbe EP environment

EP in Dushanbe provides a structured security overlay for the principal movement cycle, calibrated to the current threat assessment covering border proximity risks, political sensitivity, and petty crime in market areas. Source: FCDO Tajikistan travel advice (2026).

## What executive protection covers in Dushanbe

An EP programme in Dushanbe covers the full principal movement cycle: advance work at all venues, close escort during movements, accommodation security liaison, daily route variation, a documented emergency action plan, and medevac provider confirmation before arrival.
