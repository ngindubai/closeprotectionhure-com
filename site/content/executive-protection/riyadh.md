Riyadh executive protection operates at the intersection of three distinct risk dimensions: the regional conflict creating active infrastructure threat, the specific legal environment for foreign nationals, and the standard corporate security requirements of a major business destination. Professional EP in Riyadh addresses all three as integrated components rather than treating physical security as the only relevant element.

## The EP requirement in Riyadh

For most senior executive visitors to Riyadh, the EP engagement starts with a written threat assessment covering all three dimensions, a pre-travel legal environment brief for the principal and any accompanying staff, MoI-authorised security driver and CP coverage, and shelter-in-place protocol awareness. For principals in sectors with specific regional sensitivity, defence, technology, financial services with sanctions exposure, a sector-specific dimension is added.

Riyadh's ambient crime environment for foreign business visitors is low. The threats that drive EP demand here are specific: the Houthi regional dimension, the legal environment risk, and for specific sectors, the intelligence environment.

## MoI authorisation framework

Formal commercial EP in Saudi Arabia operates through MoI-authorised Saudi partners. Foreign CPOs work in coordination with those partners. The verification step is the MoI authorisation number of the Saudi operating company.
