Executive protection in Istanbul is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against terrorism exposure and political instability: advance work on venues and routes, a written threat assessment, an operations controller, and discreet secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.

## The EP requirement in Istanbul

For senior executive visitors to Istanbul, the EP baseline is an OGG-certified CPO with IST or SAW collection protocol, Bosphorus geography planning, operations controller coverage, and a written pre-travel threat assessment covering the current terrorism threat status. For principals in defence, technology, and energy sectors, counter-surveillance awareness is added as a standard component rather than an optional extra.

Istanbul is a functioning major business city. EP here is about calibrated awareness rather than fortress mentality: the terrorism threat is real and requires informed planning, but it does not prevent professional corporate operations.

## OGG licensing for EP in Istanbul

OGG certification under Law 5188 is the regulatory requirement for EP in Turkey. Armed cover requires additional Ministry of Interior firearms authorisation. Foreign close protection personnel cannot carry firearms in Turkey. The verification step is the OGG certificate number of the specific officers proposed and the operating company's Ministry registration.
