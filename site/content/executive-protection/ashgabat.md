Executive protection in Ashgabat is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against government surveillance and access and visa restrictions: advance work on venues and routes, a written risk assessment, an operations controller, and approved secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where discretion is a leading design factor given the regulatory environment.

## The Ashgabat EP environment

EP in Ashgabat provides a structured security overlay for the principal movement cycle, calibrated to the current threat assessment covering heavy state surveillance, movement restrictions outside Ashgabat, and the regulatory constraints of operating in a highly controlled authoritarian environment. Source: FCDO Turkmenistan travel advice (2026).

## What executive protection covers in Ashgabat

An EP programme in Ashgabat covers the full principal movement cycle: advance work at all venues, close escort during movements, accommodation security liaison, daily route variation, a documented emergency action plan, and medevac provider confirmation before arrival.
