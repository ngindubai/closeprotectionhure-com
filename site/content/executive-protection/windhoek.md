Executive protection in Windhoek is a programme, not a single officer. It is the discipline a company applies to protect a travelling executive against vehicle theft and robbery in less-frequented areas: advance work on venues and routes, a written risk assessment, an operations controller, and licensed secure transport integrated with the corporate itinerary. This page is for security managers designing protection around a C-suite visit, where the need is layered, documented risk management rather than ad-hoc cover.
