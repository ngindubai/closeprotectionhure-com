Izmir is Turkey's major Aegean port and 3rd largest city. FCDO Turkey 2026 rates it at Level 2, placing it at substantially lower risk than southeast Turkey and broadly comparable to many European commercial cities. Executive protection in Izmir is built around terrorism awareness advance work, demonstration calendar management, and TNP-permitted operator deployment - providing appropriate professional coverage for corporate principals and HNWIs operating on the Aegean coast.

## Operating in Izmir

Alsancak and Konak form the primary corporate and hotel district, with demonstration risk on known political calendar dates. Karsiyaka provides the lower-risk residential alternative. Cesme is the HNWI coastal leisure destination, covered within the Izmir EP assignment. Advance work covers all venues, routes, and demonstration schedules before the principal arrives.
