Perth is Western Australia's capital and the administrative and commercial centre of Australia's resources and mining economy. The city hosts a significant volume of corporate events tied to the resources sector, including investor days, executive summits, and major industry conferences such as the Diggers and Dealers Mining Forum held annually in Kalgoorlie. The Perth Convention and Exhibition Centre and Crown Perth entertainment complex are the city's primary corporate event infrastructure.

## The security profile

WA Police Force Crime Statistics 2024 confirm Perth as one of Australia's lower-risk major cities for event security in general terms. The specific security considerations in Perth are driven less by elevated ambient crime and more by the nature of the events the city hosts. Resources sector executives from major international mining and energy companies travel with commercially sensitive schedules, significant personal profiles, and, in some cases, specific threat profiles from activist or investor-pressure campaigns. Close protection and executive transfer management are a standard planning requirement for this client segment in Perth.

The Crown Perth complex at Burswood introduces a distinct consideration: as an integrated entertainment venue, it combines a large general-public footfall with private corporate event spaces. Managing the interface between public and private areas at Crown events requires access control planning that is more sophisticated than at dedicated convention centres.

## Planning for Perth events

The WA Security and Related Activities (Control) Act 1996 provides the licensing framework for all security personnel in Western Australia. Our planning process includes early engagement with venue security teams at the PCEC and Crown Perth, with particular attention to credential management systems for the large multi-day conferences common in the resources sector.

Transfer management between Perth Airport and city venues is operationally straightforward. For resources sector principals with elevated profiles, we plan transfers to avoid predictable routines, apply appropriate vehicle selection, and co-ordinate arrival timing with venue security teams in advance.
