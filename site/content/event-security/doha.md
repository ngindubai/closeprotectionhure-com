Doha has developed into a significant international events destination, particularly following the 2022 FIFA World Cup, which produced lasting improvements to the city's security infrastructure, crowd management capacity, and inter-agency coordination. The operating environment is low risk for corporate events: violent crime is rare, and the Ministry of Interior maintains effective oversight of all security operators and large gatherings.

All private security services in Qatar must be delivered through MoI-approved operators. Our Doha teams work through established local partners with direct MoI relationships, managing event licencing, delegate briefings on local legal requirements, and on-the-day security operations at the Qatar National Convention Centre and other major venues. For executives and senior delegates, close protection details operate between Hamad International Airport and event venues throughout the engagement.
