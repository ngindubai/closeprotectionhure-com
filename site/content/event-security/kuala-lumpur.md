Kuala Lumpur is one of Southeast Asia's principal corporate events destinations, with the KLCC Convention Centre handling major international congresses and exhibitions throughout the year. Malaysia's PSA 2017 and the Private Security Industry (Licensing) Regulations 2020 set the legal framework for all commercial security work, with licences issued by the Royal Malaysia Police. The terrorism threat is assessed at MODERATE: PDRM disrupted ISIS-linked plots targeting the city in 2015 and 2016, and event security planning in Kuala Lumpur must incorporate counter-terrorism measures as a baseline requirement.

Our teams are PSA 2017-licensed and experienced across the KLCC zone's major venues. PDRM coordination, access control, credentialling, and counter-terrorism assessments are standard components of every event mandate. For executives and high-profile delegates, close protection is available from Kuala Lumpur International Airport through to post-event departure, including secure transport across the city's road network.
