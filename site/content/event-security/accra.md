Accra has emerged as a preferred host city for international conferences and ECOWAS-related meetings, partly because Ghana's comparatively stable political environment offers a more predictable operating context than many regional alternatives. The Accra International Conference Centre, Kempinski Hotel Gold Coast City and Labadi Beach Hotel are the primary properties for summit-level events. Ghana's Private Security Organisations Act 2020 establishes the licensing framework for private security companies operating in the country, and we engage only Act-compliant operators for guarding, crowd management and close protection roles in Accra. For a fuller picture of the security environment, see our [Accra city security overview](/cities/accra/).

Event security at medium-risk level still requires structured planning. While Accra does not carry the same acute threat profile as some other West African capitals, proportionate access control, credential verification and close protection for senior delegates are standard components of any well-managed international conference. Our teams coordinate with the Ghana Police Service for events at high-profile venues and ensure that emergency response protocols are agreed and rehearsed before each event begins.
