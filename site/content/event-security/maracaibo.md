It presents one of the most demanding security environments for international business travel in the Western Hemisphere. FCDO Venezuela travel advice (2024) advises against all but essential travel, citing very high violent crime, kidnapping, armed robbery and significant territorial control by organised criminal groups including colectivos. This is not a city where conventional open-access events can currently be planned. The security assessment for Maracaibo in detail is covered on the [Maracaibo city guide](/cities/maracaibo/).
