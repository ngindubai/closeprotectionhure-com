Glasgow is Scotland's largest city and the UK's leading live music and events destination outside London. The Scottish Event Campus on the Clydeside is one of Europe's busiest purpose-built events complexes, hosting major international conferences, trade shows, and concerts at the SEC Centre, SEC Armadillo, and OVO Hydro. The city also hosted COP26 in 2021 and has established infrastructure and experience for large-scale international events with significant security requirements.

## The security profile

Police Scotland Crime Statistics 2024 confirm Glasgow as a well-managed city for major events by UK standards. The terrorism threat level assessed by the UK Home Office is Substantial as of 2026, which is the background security environment for all major public events in the UK, including those in Glasgow. This does not mean that Glasgow events face specific elevated threats, but it does mean that event security planning in the UK appropriately incorporates counter-terrorism awareness protocols for large gatherings, external event areas, and VIP movements.

Glasgow's major events infrastructure is well-served by experienced SIA-licensed security operators with specific experience in the SEC campus environment. Police Scotland maintains active event liaison capability and co-operates professionally with private security providers at large-format events.

## Planning for Glasgow events

The Private Security Industry Act 2001 and SIA licensing framework are mandatory for regulated security roles in the UK. There is no exception or workaround: operators in regulated roles without current SIA licences cannot legally work at Glasgow events. Our planning process starts with licensing verification, followed by early engagement with SEC security management or venue security teams at the specific location.

Transfer management between Glasgow Airport (GLA) and the SEC or city centre hotels via the M8 is operationally straightforward in normal conditions. For events with significant VIP movements, we apply standard UK close protection transfer protocols including route assessment, timing management, and contingency route identification.
