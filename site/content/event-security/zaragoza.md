Two very different kinds of event drive most of the demand for event security in [Zaragoza](/cities/zaragoza/): trade fairs and business conferences held at Feria de Zaragoza, and the enormous public gathering of Fiestas del Pilar every October, when the city takes in over one million visitors across around ten days. They call for different planning entirely, and a page that tried to treat them the same would not be much use to anyone actually organising something here.

Feria de Zaragoza work tends to be about structure: controlled access points, exhibitor and stand protection, loading bay security during build and breakdown, and coordination with the venue's own security team so nothing overlaps or contradicts. A fair share of the trade events held there connect to Zaragoza's automotive and logistics economy, PLAZA and the Stellantis Figueruelas plant both draw exhibitors and buyers with a corporate profile, and those events often need delegate close protection and secure movement, not just gate control. Fiestas del Pilar is a different problem: it is open, crowded, and concentrated around Coso and Plaza del Pilar, and any private function running during that window needs transfer timing and route planning built around genuinely restricted vehicle access, not the usual assumptions about how long a journey across the city takes.

Delegate arrivals for either kind of event usually come through ZAZ Zaragoza Airport, which is where our [secure airport transfers work](/secure-airport-transfers/zaragoza/) plugs directly into the event security plan, and for a delegate whose profile warrants individual protection rather than a general security presence, that sits alongside our dedicated [close protection officer service](/close-protection-officers/zaragoza/). We build every Zaragoza event plan from the same current picture: the documented pickpocketing pattern in Casco Historico, El Tubo, and around Zaragoza Delicias station, the possibility of civil demonstrations in the same public spaces used by festival crowds, and the chance that industrial action at Stellantis Figueruelas disrupts regional transport routes with limited notice.
