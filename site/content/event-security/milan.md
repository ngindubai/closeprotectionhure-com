Milan is one of Europe's principal cities for trade exhibitions, luxury events and international conferences, drawing hundreds of thousands of delegates annually to venues such as Fiera Milano and MiCo - Milan Convention Centre. Italy's private security sector is tightly regulated under the Testo Unico delle Leggi di Pubblica Sicurezza (TULPS), which requires security companies and individual operatives to hold Prefettura-issued licences before operating in the region. Our partner network in Milan comprises only TULPS-compliant Istituti di Vigilanza Privata, and all event security plans are submitted to the Questura in accordance with Italian public event regulations. For a broader picture of the operating environment, see our [Milan city security overview](/cities/milan/).

Event profiles in Milan vary considerably. Milan Fashion Week attracts global media and high-net-worth attendees across dozens of private and semi-public show venues throughout the city. EICMA and Salone del Mobile generate very large footprints at Fiera Milano requiring structured crowd flow management, multi-zone access credentialing and coordinated emergency response planning. Our event security teams apply the same core methodology across all formats: venue risk assessment, personnel deployment scaled to attendance, and real-time command communication. For organisations requiring personal protection alongside event coverage, our [event security service overview](/services/event-security/) sets out how close protection integrates with venue security teams.
