Amsterdam is a major international congress city, with the RAI Amsterdam Convention Centre hosting large-scale trade fairs and conferences throughout the year. The security environment is moderate by Western European standards: the NCTV maintains a SUBSTANTIAL terrorism threat rating and vehicle exclusion barriers are standard across the city centre following the post-2016 reassessment of crowded-place security in the Netherlands.

All event security personnel must hold Wpbr registration under Dutch law, and events above defined attendance thresholds require an evenementenvergunning from Amsterdam municipality. Our teams manage both legal compliance and operational security planning, from venue threat assessments through to post-event stand-down. For high-profile corporate events, close protection and secure transport from Schiphol are available alongside the wider event security package.
