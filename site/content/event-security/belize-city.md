Belize City is the largest city in Belize and the centre of the country's maritime, fisheries, tourism, and offshore financial services sectors. The Radisson Fort George Hotel and Best Western Biltmore Plaza in the Fort George district are the principal venues for international business events, offering a manageable operating environment within a city that carries elevated gang-related crime in its southern districts. Belize's English-speaking environment and its legal and financial services sector make it an active venue for offshore financial conferences and investment events.

## The Belize City event security environment

The FCDO advises against all but essential travel to certain areas of Belize City, including the Southside residential districts, while noting that the Fort George area is manageable for international visitors. The US State Department classifies Belize at Level 2 (Exercise Increased Caution), specifically noting crime in Belize City. Gang activity is the primary driver of serious crime. Hurricane season (May to November) adds a weather contingency planning requirement that is unique among this region's event security considerations. Source: FCDO Belize travel advice (2026); US State Dept Belize Level 2 advisory (2026).

## Planning events in Belize City

Venue selection for international events in Belize City centres on the Fort George hotel district. SIA-licensed operators are engaged for security functions. Hurricane season events require integrated contingency planning for weather disruption. The 15km airport transfer from Goldson International requires pre-arranged vetted transport.
