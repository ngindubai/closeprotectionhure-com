Vienna is a distinctive event destination combining a high volume of international corporate congresses with a substantial diplomatic calendar, owing to its status as host city for multiple UN agencies including UNIDO and IAEA. The security environment is assessed as low risk by Central European standards: Austria's overall crime rate is low and the FCDO advises normal travel. The 2 November 2020 Vienna attack, in which 4 people were killed near the opera quarter, nonetheless raised the profile of public-gathering security in the city.

All commercial security personnel in Austria must hold the Bewachungsgewerbe Befähigungsprüfung under the Gewerbeordnung. Our Vienna teams are fully qualified and experienced across the city's three major venue types: Messe Wien for trade fairs and congresses, the Hofburg for senior diplomatic and corporate events, and the Vienna International Centre for intergovernmental conferences. Executive protection and secure transport are available for the full duration of any event mandate.
