Event security in Bucharest is governed by Romania's Law 333/2003 on the Guard of Objectives, Goods, Values and Personal Protection framework. Bucharest hosts corporate, financial, and sector-specific conferences at Romexpo Exhibition Centre and JW Marriott Bucharest Grand Hotel infrastructure. ## Romania regulatory framework

All event security personnel at Bucharest events hold current Romania credentials under Law 333/2003 on the Guard of Objectives, Goods, Values and Personal Protection. Romanian Ministry of Internal Affairs (Ministerul Afacerilor Interne) provides oversight.
