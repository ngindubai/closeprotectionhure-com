Cairo is Egypt's primary corporate events destination and hosts significant government and regional summits at the Cairo International Conference Centre and major hotel venues. The security environment is assessed as high risk: terrorism has been active in Egypt, concentrated in Sinai and historically in Cairo, and the FCDO and US State Dept both maintain advisory warnings for Egypt as a whole. Corporate events in Cairo city are feasible with appropriate security arrangements, but require more intensive planning than events in lower-risk destinations.

All private security services in Egypt must be coordinated through the Ministry of Interior, and major events require police liaison as a matter of course. Our Cairo teams work through MoI-approved local operators and manage police coordination, credentialling, and on-the-day incident command. For executives and senior delegates, close protection is available from Cairo International Airport throughout the event, with route planning that accounts for current MoI intelligence on the operating environment.
