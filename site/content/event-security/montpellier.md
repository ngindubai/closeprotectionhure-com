Montpellier has held French Tech Capital status since 2019, and IBM has operated a major site in the city since 1965, and that pairing sets the tone for a corporate event calendar built around the Corum's congress halls rather than a single flagship trade fair. Multi-day conferences need sustained credential management and vehicle staging that a one-day reception does not, while the biotech and life sciences activity tied to Sanofi's manufacturing and research presence adds a second, more discreet strand of closed-session sector events.

CNAPS, France's national private security regulator, licenses both firms and individual officers under the Code de la securite interieure, and any provider working a Montpellier engagement should be able to confirm current authorisation on request. Delegates typically arrive through Montpellier-Mediterranee Airport or by TGV into Gare Saint-Roch, where local reporting has documented organised pickpocket and card-theft activity, which is why our standard collection point sits away from the station concourse rather than at street level.

France's Vigipirate Urgence Attentat posture applies nationally, and FCDO France advises heightened vigilance at transport hubs and crowded public areas, a baseline that informs perimeter planning at any high-footfall Corum event. La Paillade, in the Mosson district, is an officially designated Zone de Securite Prioritaire and is excluded from route planning regardless of the event type, and periodic national strike days can affect the Ecusson and Comedie square with limited notice.
