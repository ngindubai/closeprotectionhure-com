Berlin is one of Europe's most active conference and trade fair cities, with Messe Berlin regularly hosting major international congresses and exhibitions. The security environment is assessed at moderate risk: Germany maintains a HIGH terrorism threat rating from the BfV following multiple attack plots and the 2016 Christmas market vehicle attack. All private security staff operating at events in Berlin must hold the §34a Gewerbeordnung DIHK sachkunde certificate, and large events require advance coordination with Polizei Berlin.

Vehicle exclusion has become a standard component of event perimeter planning in the city since December 2016. Our teams work with venue management and Polizei Berlin to specify barrier types, confirm access routes, and ensure vehicle exclusion zones are correctly implemented. For diplomatic events and high-profile corporate gatherings, close protection details are available for speakers and senior executives.
