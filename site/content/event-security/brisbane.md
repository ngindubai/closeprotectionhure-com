Brisbane is Queensland's capital and South East Queensland's primary corporate event destination, supported by the BCEC at South Bank, the Brisbane Showgrounds precinct, and a strong city hotel infrastructure. The city's selection as host of the 2032 Olympic Games has driven sustained investment in events infrastructure and has positioned Brisbane as a significant international conference destination alongside Sydney and Melbourne.

## The security profile

QPS Crime Statistics 2024 confirm Brisbane as one of Australia's lower-risk major cities for event security. The practical risk profile for corporate event planners centres on professional planning disciplines rather than active threat environments: access control for large delegate numbers, transfer management for arriving and departing executives, and close protection for high-profile speakers. The South Bank precinct, home to the BCEC, is a well-managed public-private environment with established security infrastructure and good co-operation from venue teams.

Brisbane's event calendar includes major sporting events, international cultural festivals, and large-scale industry conferences, particularly in the mining, agriculture, and resources sectors that underpin Queensland's economy. These events can involve significant media attention and executive principals that require close protection planning alongside standard event security provision.

## Planning for Brisbane events

The Queensland Security Providers Act 1993 framework is well established, and the Brisbane market has a good supply of experienced, licensed event security operators. Our planning process includes early engagement with venue teams at the BCEC and Brisbane Showgrounds, with particular attention to credentialing systems for large-format international conferences.

Transfer management between Brisbane Airport and CBD venues via the Gateway Motorway is straightforward in operational terms. Our standard planning includes pre-arrival route assessment, vehicle selection appropriate to the principal's profile, and timing management for transfers around peak hour on the Gateway and Pacific motorway corridors.
