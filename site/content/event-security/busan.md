Busan is South Korea's second-largest city and the country's principal maritime gateway, home to one of the world's largest container ports. The city hosts significant international events across the maritime, logistics, film, and technology sectors, with BEXCO in the Haeundae district serving as the primary convention and exhibition infrastructure. The Busan International Film Festival (BIFF) in October brings global media attention to the city annually.

## The security profile

National Police Agency of Korea Crime Statistics 2024 confirm South Korea as a low general-crime environment. For corporate event planners, the security considerations in Busan are shaped by the international character of the events the city hosts rather than by ambient crime. Senior executives from major global shipping, port logistics, and maritime companies travel with commercially sensitive schedules and, for some, significant public profiles. The maritime sector's concentration of commercially sensitive cargo and trade information adds a specific dimension to access control planning for maritime industry events.

BEXCO hosts international events with delegate groups from across Asia, Europe, and North America. Multi-national delegate management, multi-language credentialing, and co-ordination with Korean Police Agency event protocols are standard planning elements for large-format international conferences at the venue.

## Planning for Busan events

The Korea Guard Services Act framework is administered by the National Police Agency and provides clear operator licensing requirements. Our planning process includes early engagement with venue security teams at BEXCO, Lotte Hotel Busan, and Haeundae Grand Hotel. Transfer management between Gimhae International Airport and Haeundae is the standard airport route; for delegates arriving via KTX from Seoul's main stations, we include station transfer management in the event security plan.

Busan's Haeundae district concentrates the city's major hotels, BEXCO, and the primary BIFF festival venues in a relatively compact area, which simplifies multi-venue movement planning. We build integrated transfer schedules for events that require delegate movements across multiple Haeundae venues within a single day.
