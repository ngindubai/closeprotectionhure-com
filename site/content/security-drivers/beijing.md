## PSB licensing and the China regulatory framework

China's Regulations on Security Service (2009) require all commercial security operators to hold PSB (Public Security Bureau) operating licences at the relevant administrative level. In Beijing, the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau issues operating licences. Foreign companies cannot independently provide security services; all commercial security driving in Beijing operates through licensed Chinese partners. This legal requirement is strictly enforced.

## The odd-even plate restriction: a Beijing operational fact

Beijing's licence plate restriction system -- odd-numbered plates on odd calendar days, even on even days, during weekday peak hours -- is a logistical variable that every Beijing security driver operation must account for. Professional operating companies maintain dual plate fleets or confirmed workarounds. Any operator who has not anticipated this constraint is not operationally prepared for Beijing.
