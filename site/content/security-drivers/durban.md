## The Durban driving environment

The N2 is the primary movement axis for Umhlanga, King Shaka Airport, La Lucia Ridge, and Durban CBD. Peak congestion at 07:00-09:00 and 16:30-18:30 adds 15-20 minutes to most transfers. Counter-carjacking driving protocols apply at every robot on every journey. The M4 coastal route is pre-surveyed as a standard N2 alternative.
