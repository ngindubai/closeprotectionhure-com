The FCDO rates Italy as broadly low-risk but advises heightened vigilance around stations and busy plazas. In Turin, Porta Nuova station and Piazza San Carlo present the primary pickpocketing concentrations. Industrial and political demonstrations periodically assemble in Piazza Castello, requiring short-notice route adjustment.
