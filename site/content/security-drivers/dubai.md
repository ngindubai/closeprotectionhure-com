## The ground transport context in Dubai

Violent crime against business visitors in Dubai's central districts is rare. The value of a professional security driver in Dubai is primarily in reliability, accountability, and preparedness rather than in immediate threat mitigation. The pre-arranged collection protocol at DXB or DWC eliminates the logistical uncertainty of arrival. A named driver with a specific registered vehicle removes the ambiguity that unverified transport creates. Operations controller check-ins provide assurance throughout the itinerary.

For principals in sectors with regional intelligence interest, defence, sensitive technology, financial services with sanctions exposure, the secondary value of a known, vetted driver who has not been subjected to influence operations is real, though rarely the presenting reason clients request the service.

## SIRA compliance

Commercial security driving in Dubai requires SIRA licensing. Operating companies and individual personnel must hold current SIRA authorisation. The verification step is to ask for the SIRA licence numbers of the operating company and the specific driver assigned.
