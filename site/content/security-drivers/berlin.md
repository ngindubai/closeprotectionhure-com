## Section 34a and PBefG compliance

Germany's security driver regulatory framework combines two requirements: the Section 34a Sachkundeprufung for the security function, administered by the relevant IHK, and compliance with the Personenbeforderungsgesetz (PBefG) for the commercial transport function. Both are confirmable and required for fully compliant security driving in Germany. Operating companies registered with the Berlin IHK and compliant with PBefG provide the appropriate legal basis for commercial security driving in Berlin.
