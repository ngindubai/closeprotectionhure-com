Violent crime directly targeting foreign business visitors during ground movement in Mumbai is not a primary pattern. Traffic unpredictability on these routes makes departure-time planning and route alternatives operationally relevant.

## PSARA compliance

Commercial security operations in India are PSARA-regulated at state level. For Mumbai, the relevant authority is the Maharashtra Controlling Authority. Drivers must be employed by a Maharashtra PSARA-licensed agency. The verification step is to ask for the Maharashtra PSARA licence number of the operating company.
