Hannover Messe changes the shape of every logistics plan for the week it runs, typically in April. Deutsche Messe AG's flagship event is described industry-wide as the world's largest industrial trade fair, with more than 4,000 exhibitors from over 70 countries attending, and hotel and transfer capacity across the whole city tightens sharply as a result. A driver who hasn't confirmed vehicles well ahead of fair week will find very little available on the day.

Beyond the fair, Hannover's corporate geography has shifted in a useful way for ground transport. Continental AG opened its new global headquarters campus, Continental-Plaza, in December 2023, and it offers straightforward, well-managed vehicle access compared with older industrial sites. Hannover Airport, about 11 kilometres north in Langenhagen, connects to Hauptbahnhof via the S5 S-Bahn in roughly 17 minutes, a short run outside fair periods.

Two things need closer attention. Hannover Hauptbahnhof recorded 1,606 offences in the first five months of 2025 according to Bundespolizei figures, though a joint police and city initiative running through September 2025 cut violent crime there by more than 20%; drivers still use designated pickup points rather than general kerbside waiting. And Wolfsburg, home to Volkswagen Group's global headquarters, sits close enough (about 90 kilometres, roughly 30 minutes by direct ICE train, or a comparable run via the A2) that VW-linked business frequently extends a Hannover itinerary into a genuine two-city day.
