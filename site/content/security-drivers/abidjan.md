## Licensing and compliance in Ivory Coast

Security companies must hold Ministry of Interior authorisation, and individual drivers must carry current credentials from the relevant authority. FCDO describes the framework as developing; verification before deployment is essential. Foreign operators work through approved Ivorian partners.

## Abidjan ground movement considerations

Every ABJ transfer to Le Plateau, Cocody, or Zone 4 involves at least one bridge crossing. Security drivers select bridge routes based on current traffic and police activity, minimise night movement, and apply anti-carjacking protocols at all vehicle stops.
