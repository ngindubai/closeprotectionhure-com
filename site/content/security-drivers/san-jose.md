San Jose's position as Costa Rica's capital and the heart of the country's technology and medical devices sector brings a consistent flow of multinational executives, investors and senior managers through the city. The overall risk environment is the most favourable in Central America: Costa Rica has maintained political stability since abolishing its standing army in 1948, and the Ministry of Security operates a more developed private security regulatory framework than any neighbouring state.

## The ground transport case in San Jose

The value of a professional security driver in San Jose is primarily about reliability, consistency and appropriate threat reduction rather than protection from acute violence. Vehicle break-ins targeting hire cars are the most commonly reported property crime for corporate visitors, and these are directly mitigated by a non-hire-car vehicle profile and a driver who applies counter-surveillance discipline throughout the itinerary. The SJO airport to Escazu transfer is the highest-exposure period for most corporate arrivals, and pre-arranged named collection eliminates the unverified transport risk that this transition otherwise creates.

## Escazu operations

Escazu is the base of operations for most San Jose security driving assignments. The district hosts the majority of technology sector offices, international hotel brands and senior executive residences used by multinational organisations. Drivers assigned to Escazu itineraries are familiar with the specific layout and security considerations of the principal office parks and hotel properties, which matters for timing, access and vehicle positioning.

## Caribbean coast awareness

For any movements towards the Caribbean coast corridor, including Limon and the approach routes, additional routing care is applied. Drug trafficking spillover activity is higher in this corridor than in the Central Valley, and time-of-day planning for those legs is built into the movement plan in advance.
