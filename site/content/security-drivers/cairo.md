## Egyptian regulatory framework

Private security companies in Cairo are licensed by the Ministry of Interior's Central Administration for Private Security. Individual security personnel hold Ministry of Interior authorisations. Clients should confirm both company-level licensing and driver-level authorisation, particularly for assignments near government premises or diplomatic facilities.
