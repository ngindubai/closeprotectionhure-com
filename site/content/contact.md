## Confidential from the first contact

Every enquiry is treated as sensitive. We do not log enquiries on shared systems, share your details with operators without your consent, or follow up with marketing after a conversation ends.

If your requirement is time-sensitive, say so. We prioritise urgent requests.

## What to include in your enquiry

You do not need a fully formed brief. A destination, rough dates, and a sense of what you need is enough to start a conversation. We will ask follow-up questions where necessary.

Useful information to include:

- Destination city and travel dates
- Nature of the visit (corporate, personal, media, NGO)
- Number of principals requiring protection
- Specific concerns (profile sensitivity, known threats, previous incidents)
- Services you believe you need (close protection, security driver, residential, event)
- Your preferred response method (encrypted email, phone, secure call)

## No pricing on this page

We do not publish pricing. Every requirement is different. A single close protection officer for a day visit to Dubai is not the same as a multi-operator detail for a two-week assignment in Karachi.

All pricing is provided in a written proposal after we understand your specific requirements. There is no obligation.
