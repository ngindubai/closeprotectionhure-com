## The residential environment in Paramaribo

Suriname's gold mining, bauxite, and oil sectors draw international personnel primarily from the Netherlands, United States, and other Caribbean nations. Paramaribo's historic inner city is a UNESCO World Heritage site, and residential properties in the riverside districts combine security with proximity to Suriname's business and government centre. The primary residential security concerns in Paramaribo are property crime and the need for generator-backed systems given periodic power interruptions. Armed guarding is not typically required for the majority of expatriate residential properties at Suriname's current risk level.
