Security personnel must hold a Tarjeta de Identificacion Profesional (TIP). The law distinguishes between vigilante de seguridad (armed, requires specific authorisation) and escolta privado (close protection escort); residential security services typically operate as unarmed provision supplemented by technical systems unless there is a specific risk profile warranting armed personnel.
