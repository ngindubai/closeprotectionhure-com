Maputo residential security is calibrated to Mozambique's high-risk profile and the energy sector operating framework that has shaped the Maputo expatriate security model: Ministry of Interior-licensed operators delivering compound security assessments, domestic staff vetting, armed robbery protocols, and medevac-ready emergency planning.

## The residential environment in Maputo

Maputo's residential environment for senior expatriates and energy sector personnel centres on the Sommerschield and Polana Cimento zones, which provide the most consistent security infrastructure and services in the city. Armed robbery and vehicle crime are documented across Maputo's zones; the residential programme addresses these through property assessment, vetted transport protocols, and armed response integration.

The growth of Mozambique's liquefied natural gas sector has brought international energy company security frameworks into the Maputo operating environment, raising the baseline standard for commercial residential security operators in the city.

## What residential security covers in Maputo

A Maputo residential security programme includes a written property assessment, Ministry of Interior-licensed guard deployment and armed response integration, domestic staff vetting through the national identity and PRM process, armed robbery and vehicle crime protocol for domestic movement, and a medevac-ready emergency protocol covering Maputo Private Hospital and Johannesburg or Nairobi evacuation capacity.
