What connects the two isn't the threat profile so much as the terrain: steep gradients, narrow access roads, and properties that don't always sit at road level. Our residential surveys start there, because a security plan built for flat ground fails quietly in a hillside city until the day it matters.

Any CCTV installed at the property has to sit within EU GDPR and Italy's Codice Privacy framework, with the Garante's guidance on retention and signage applied properly rather than assumed to not apply because it's a private home.
