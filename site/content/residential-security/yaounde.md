## The residential environment in Yaounde

Yaounde's residential security environment for international personnel is anchored in the Bastos district. Within Bastos, the main risks are petty theft and opportunistic crime rather than the more serious violence that affects central Yaounde and the outlying areas. The national context requires that staff vetting addresses regional conflict connections, and that travel planning for any staff member who needs to move between cities within Cameroon explicitly avoids the FCDO against-travel zones in the Far North, Northwest, and Southwest. Generator backup for security systems is a standard programme element given Yaounde's power supply reliability.
