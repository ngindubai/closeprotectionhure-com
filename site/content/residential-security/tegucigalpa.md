## The residential environment in Tegucigalpa

Tegucigalpa's topography of hills and valleys creates natural separation between its residential zones and the higher-crime areas. The Lomas del Guijarro and Palmira areas sit at higher elevation in the eastern part of the city and have the best available security infrastructure for expatriate residential use: gated compounds with perimeter walls, CCTV, and 24-hour guarding are standard features in this zone. Extortion networks that target businesses also sometimes extend to residential properties, making domestic staff vetting and routine avoidance essential components of any residential programme.
