## The residential context in Istanbul

Physical crime targeting residential properties in Istanbul's central expat areas is documented but not among the highest in the cities we cover. The terrorism threat, while primarily targeting crowded public venues, is the context for the household awareness protocol that professional residential security in Istanbul now includes. The intelligence environment, relevant for expats in defence, energy, and technology sectors, is part of the assessment for those profiles.

Bosphorus-facing residential properties have a specific character: they are typically higher-value, attract more attention from opportunistic crime, and have specific access and evacuation considerations related to the waterfront geography.

## OGG compliance for residential security

Turkey's Law 5188 requires OGG certification for commercial security personnel including residential guards. Operating companies must also hold Ministry of Interior registration. The verification step is the OGG certificate of building security staff and the Ministry registration of the operating company.
