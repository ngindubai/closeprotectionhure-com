## The residential environment in Managua

Managua's residential security environment reflects two distinct risk layers. The first is conventional: property crime, robbery, and limited emergency response capacity in parts of the city. The second is political: documented surveillance of foreign nationals and international organisations, arbitrary detention risk, and the suppression of civil society create a specific operating challenge for international staff based in the city. Generator backup for all security-critical systems is an essential programme element given Nicaragua's power reliability issues.

## What residential security covers in Managua

A Managua residential programme covers DGPN-licensed compound guarding, generator-backed access control and CCTV, domestic staff vetting with police clearance and reference checks, and documented emergency response and evacuation planning to MGA airport.
