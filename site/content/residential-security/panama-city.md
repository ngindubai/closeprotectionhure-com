Panama City's residential security environment reflects the city's dual identity: as Central America's primary financial hub, it attracts HNWI residents, senior banking executives and corporate principals who require ongoing residential protection planning beyond the standard building security arrangements. The medium risk picture, carjacking on approach routes, organised crime activity in peripheral areas and periodic civil unrest, means that residential security assessment is a practical tool for corporate and HNWI households, not an overreaction.

## Residential zones

Punta Pacifica, Punta Paitilla, Miraflores and San Francisco are Panama City's primary residential zones for expatriate and HNWI households. High-rise residential developments in these areas typically have monitored building security, concierge access control and dedicated parking. A professional residential security assessment reviews these baseline arrangements and identifies the gaps that standard building security does not address: keyholder management, domestic staff access, vehicle approach protocols and emergency planning.

## The carjacking dimension

Carjacking and armed robbery are documented risks on certain Panama City corridors. For residential households, this translates into specific assessment work: the vehicle access approach to the property, gate and barrier security, the household's protocol for confirming identity before admitting vehicles, and documented alternative routing for regular journeys from the residence. These are low-cost measures that are frequently absent without a structured assessment.

## Civil unrest preparedness

The 2022 and 2023 civil unrest that disrupted Panama's ground transport network for extended periods is a standing reference for residential security planning. The civil unrest protocol for a residential household covers shelter-in-place capability, emergency provisions, communication channels that remain operable if normal routes are disrupted, and awareness of the protest calendar and blockade locations near the residence.
