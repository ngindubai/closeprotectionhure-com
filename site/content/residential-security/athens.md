## Greece regulatory framework

Greece residential security operates under Presidential Decree 78/2011 on Security Guards Licensing, with Hellenic Police (Elliniki Astynomia) and Ministry of Citizen Protection (YPDM) providing licensing oversight. GDPR and the national data-protection authority framework apply to residential CCTV and domestic staff personal data. All deployed operators hold current Greece-compliant credentials.
