Operational detail for Panama City

Tocumen International Airport Collection Protocol

Tocumen International Airport (PTY) is located approximately 25 kilometres east of central Panama City, along a transfer corridor that passes through areas with elevated organised crime exposure. Our collection officer positions inside the arrivals hall before the principal exits baggage claim, ensuring immediate meet-and-escort without kerb exposure. No transfer uses informal or kerb-side transport at PTY.

Organised Crime-Aware Routing

Clan del Golfo affiliates operate across Panama, elevating carjacking and armed robbery risk on principal transport routes, particularly at night and on the PTY corridor. Transfer routes are selected to minimise exposure on the highest-risk highway stretches, and routing decisions are updated against current crime intelligence before each transfer. Night-time transfers receive additional operational review.

Directorate of Private Security Services Licensed Operators

Panama's Directorate of Private Security Services, under the Ministry of Government, licences all private security operators. Every transfer operator used for Panama City engagements holds current directorate registration. Operator licence documentation is provided to clients before transfer. Unregistered operators are excluded from consideration regardless of cost or availability.

Protest and Road Blockade Contingency

The 2022-2023 protest cycle demonstrated that arterial routes between PTY and central Panama City can be blocked with limited warning. Every Panama City transfer includes a protest contingency route as standard, not as an elevated option. The operations controller monitors current protest activity before collection and can activate the contingency route before the principal lands.

Destination Zone Coverage

Primary transfer destinations include Punta Pacifica, Punta Paitilla, Miraflores, San Francisco, and the Casco Viejo area for daytime arrivals. All destination zones receive a pre-advance hotel liaison before the principal's flight arrives. Transfer routes and destination approach plans are adjusted for each zone based on the current security picture.

Operations Controller Throughout

An operations controller monitors every Panama City transfer from PTY collection to the principal's destination. The controller holds British Embassy Panama City emergency contacts, monitors current traffic, security, and protest conditions on the PTY corridor, and communicates with the driver at defined check-in intervals. Arrival confirmation at the destination is sent to the client's travel or security team on completion.
