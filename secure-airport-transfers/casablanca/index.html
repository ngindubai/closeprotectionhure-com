What this covers

Operational detail for Casablanca

Mohammed V International Airport Collection Protocol

Mohammed V International Airport (CMN) is located approximately 30 kilometres south of Casablanca's central business district. The airport serves as Morocco's primary international gateway, with connections to European capitals, Middle East hubs, and sub-Saharan Africa. Our collection team positions inside the arrivals terminal before the principal exits customs and baggage claim. Inside-terminal collection ensures the principal is met before navigating the external taxi and informal transport area, where targeting of international arrivals by unofficial operators is documented.

CMN to Casablanca City Centre Transfer Route

The standard CMN-to-Casablanca transfer uses the A7 motorway approach to the Maarif and Gauthier business districts and Casablanca Finance City (CFC). The transfer is approximately 30 kilometres and takes 25 to 40 minutes under normal conditions. Evening peak-hour traffic on the Casablanca ring road approach can extend this to 50 to 60 minutes. The Ain Diab coastal boulevard route is an alternate providing access to the coastal hotel zone from a different approach.

Ministry of Interior Law 27-06 Licensing Framework

Morocco's private security sector is regulated by the Ministry of Interior under Law 27-06. Security companies providing transport services must hold current government licensing and individual drivers require formal accreditation. Standard security transport operates unarmed. Transfer operators for Casablanca engagements hold current Ministry of Interior authorisation, verified before each assignment. Documentation is provided to clients as part of the pre-transfer confirmation package.

Morocco Legal Environment Pre-Transfer Briefing

Every Casablanca secure transfer includes a pre-arrival legal briefing covering Morocco-specific factors: laws on drug possession (severe sentences), alcohol (licensed premises only), photography of government and military infrastructure, and the LGBTQ+ legal situation. This briefing is standard for all arriving principals who have not previously been briefed on the Moroccan legal environment and forms the baseline for the full Casablanca assignment.

Terrorism Awareness Context

Morocco's counter-terrorism services regularly disrupt Daesh-inspired cells, and the FCDO rates the terrorism threat as likely. Airport environments and major hotels are in the target category for terrorism-aware situational awareness. The arrival at CMN is managed with standard terrorism-aware protocols: avoiding loitering in crowded arrival areas, direct vehicle embarkation after collection, and the pre-selected hotel zone as the direct destination.

CFC and Ain Diab Hotel Zone Pre-Advance and Operations Controller

Pre-advance at destination hotels in Casablanca Finance City, Ain Diab, and Maarif is completed before the principal's flight arrives. The Hyatt Regency Casablanca, Sofitel Casablanca Tour Blanche, and Kenzi Tower Hotel are among the principal senior corporate hotel properties. An operations controller tracks the CMN-to-destination transfer, monitors A7 motorway conditions, and provides arrival confirmation to the client's travel team.
