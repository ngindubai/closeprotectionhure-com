Nelson Mandela International Airport (RAI) is a compact, well-organised facility located 3km from central Praia. Cape Verde benefits from one of the most stable security environments in West Africa, with the FCDO advising standard precautions (2026) and the US State Department maintaining a Level 1 designation (2026). For most visitors, the risk level does not demand elevated transfer protocols. However, HNW individuals, high-profile executives, and principals with sensitive itineraries routinely choose pre-arranged vetted transport as a matter of good practice rather than necessity, and Praia is no exception to that principle.

Our transfer service at RAI provides professional, discreet collection inside the terminal, with vetted locally licensed drivers and vehicles appropriate for the island environment. The compact size of the airport makes the process straightforward, and the short distance to the city centre means transfers are efficient. Medical contingency planning references Hospital Agostinho Neto in-city, with medevac routing to Dakar or Lisbon identified for higher-acuity situations. Principals whose itineraries extend beyond Praia can request onward coordination across Santiago Island or assistance with inter-island logistics.

For principals who require on-ground security beyond the transfer, our close protection officers in Praia provide coverage appropriate to the environment, and our bodyguard hire service in Praia can be added to any visit. The Praia city security profile gives a broader overview of the local environment for those planning their first visit.
