Operational detail for Copenhagen

Copenhagen Airport Kastrup (CPH) Collection Protocol

Copenhagen Airport Kastrup (CPH) (CPH) is located approximately 8 kilometres from Copenhagen's city centre, with a transfer time of 15 to 25 minutes under normal traffic conditions. The collection team positions inside CPH's international arrivals hall before the principal exits baggage claim. Vehicle pre-advance is completed before the principal's flight lands. Counter-surveillance awareness is applied from the arrivals hall for principals with elevated profiles.

Transfer Route and Timing

The CPH-to-Copenhagen city centre transfer covers 8 kilometres via the principal arterial approach. Transfer time is 15 to 25 minutes under normal conditions, with additional time required during morning peak (07:30 to 09:30) and evening peak (16:30 to 19:00). Transfers to Nordhavn, Osterport, and Orestaden (Amager) use the most direct routing with live traffic monitoring by the operations controller.

Denmark Transfer Operator Licensing

Secure transfer operators in Denmark work under Security Guard Act (Vagtvirksomhedsloven). The oversight body is Danish Police (Rigspolitiet) and Danish Security and Intelligence Service (PET). All Copenhagen transfer partners hold current Denmark credentials, with licence documentation provided to the client before the transfer date.

Principal Hotel Pre-Advance

Pre-advance at the principal's Copenhagen hotel (Hotel d'Angleterre Copenhagen, Nimb Hotel Copenhagen, or Marriott Copenhagen as applicable) is completed before the principal's flight arrives. The duty manager is contacted to confirm room readiness and building access. The transfer team receives hotel status confirmation before CPH departure.

Operations Controller Monitoring

An operations controller tracks the CPH-to-hotel transfer in real time. Live road condition monitoring is maintained throughout the transfer. The client's travel coordinator receives arrival confirmation when the principal reaches the hotel. Any route variation is communicated in real time.
