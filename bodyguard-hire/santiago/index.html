RNGDS-Licensed Close Protection

All security operators in Chile must hold current registration under the Registro Nacional de Guardias de Seguridad (RNGDS), administered by Carabineros de Chile. Companies providing armed operators require a separate Carabineros concession. We work exclusively through RNGDS-registered operators holding current authorisations.

Armed CP for Mining and HNWI Principals

Armed close protection is actively practised in Chile, particularly for mining sector executives, political principals and ultra-high-net-worth individuals. Armed operators must hold RNGDS authorisation for weapons carry. We coordinate armed capability as standard for assessed higher-threat engagements.

Airport Threat: SCL Taxi Scam Protocols

FCDO documentation records individual losses of up to GBP 10,000 from the Santiago airport taxi scam, where criminals in arrivals halls approach passengers with official-looking lanyards and inflate fares by adding digits. Our collection protocol eliminates this exposure: a named, pre-briefed officer meets principals in the arrivals hall before any approach is made.

Vehicle Targeting and Carjacking Awareness

FCDO advises that hire cars are specifically targeted at viewpoints and petrol stations, that a tyre puncture scam is active on approach roads, and that armed carjacking occurs on affluent highways. Route planning incorporates these patterns. Principals are briefed on the tyre puncture protocol: do not stop in an unlit area.

Civil Unrest Calendar and Protest Routing

Santiago has predictable civil unrest flashpoints: 11 September (coup anniversary), 29 March (Day of Young Combatant), 1 May (Workers Day) and Friday afternoons are historically active. Our teams carry the current protest calendar and maintain alternative routing for all principal movements during peak protest periods.

Business District and Residential Coverage

Las Condes and Vitacura form Santiago's eastern business corridor and are assessed as the safest zones for corporate and residential operations. Providencia and Nunoa are also covered. Mining company headquarters, financial district meetings and private residence coverage are all serviced from these base areas.
