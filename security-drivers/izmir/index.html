What this covers

Operational detail for Izmir

Licensing and Vehicle Standards

Private security in Turkey is governed by Law No. 5188/2004, with companies and individual personnel licensed by the Turkish National Police (Emniyet Genel Mudurlugu). Security drivers must hold current Emniyet credentials. Armoured vehicles are available through the Turkish operator network; armed vehicle escorts require specific authorisation. Clients should request sight of both company and individual licensing documentation before engagement. Foreign operators typically work through locally licensed Turkish partner firms.

Route Planning and Threat Assessment

Adnan Menderes Airport (ADB) is 18km south of central Izmir via the O-31 motorway and E87 highway. The FCDO Turkey travel advice (2024) advises enhanced precautions throughout Turkey, citing a terrorism risk including the attack at the Izmir courthouse in January 2021 attributed to TAK/PKK-affiliated individuals. Route planning for Izmir principals applies counter-surveillance techniques on airport transfers, avoids predictable government and court building approaches, and varies arrival and departure timing where operationally possible.

Fleet and Vehicle Security

Standard Izmir deployments use high-specification saloon or SUV vehicles. Given the moderate terrorism risk documented by FCDO Turkey (2024), anti-surveillance procedures are applied on all principal transfers. Armoured vehicles are recommended for principals assessed at elevated personal risk. All vehicles operate with operations-controller GPS tracking. Vehicle inspection protocols are followed before every deployment.

Driver Training and Certification

Security drivers covering Izmir hold current Emniyet credentials and are trained in defensive driving, anti-surveillance procedures, emergency vehicle handling, and first aid. Familiarity with Izmir's coastal motorway system, the Konak and Alsancak central districts, and the approach roads to the Izmir port and ferry terminals is maintained as part of local driver competency.

Airport Transfer Services

Adnan Menderes Airport (ADB) is 18km from central Izmir. Transfer cover includes inside-terminal collection, counter-surveillance departure from the airport perimeter, route-planned transfer to the principal hotel (Hilton Izmir, Marriott Izmir, Swissotel Buyuk Efes are the primary business hotels), and operations-controller tracking throughout. Ferry terminal connections at Izmir Port and Pasaport Pier for principals arriving by sea are also managed.

Emergency Protocols

All Izmir security driver operations run against the Turkish emergency baseline: 155 for police, 112 for all emergencies and ambulance, 110 for fire. Consular contacts: British Consulate Izmir +90 232 463 5151; US Consulate Izmir +90 232 455 1593. An operations controller maintains continuous GPS tracking and check-in communications throughout every transfer, with contingency protocols for loss of contact.
