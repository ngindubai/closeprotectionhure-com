German Licensing Requirements

Security drivers based in Hannover work under Sec 34a of the Gewerbeordnung together with the Bewachungsverordnung, with the IHK Sachkundeprufung required for higher-risk assignments. Firearms are rare under the Waffengesetz and Hannover deployments are unarmed as standard. This framework is consistent nationally, so a firm's registration transfers cleanly whether the job is a single Continental AG visit or a full Hannover Messe week.

Driver Training and Regional Knowledge

Drivers hold their IHK Sachkundeprufung certification and are trained on the A2 corridor toward Wolfsburg, home to Volkswagen Group's global headquarters, since drivers covering VW Group business often need to plan this as a genuine long-distance leg alongside any Hannover Messe attendance. Airport Transfers

Hannover Airport (HAJ) sits around 11 kilometres north of the centre in Langenhagen, with the S5 S-Bahn reaching Hauptbahnhof in about 17 minutes. Road transfer from HAJ is a short, well-signed run outside fair weeks. During Hannover Messe, drivers build in significantly more time given the citywide surge in transfer demand and hotel and taxi scarcity.

Emergency Protocols

Germany's emergency numbers are 110 for police and 112 for fire and ambulance. Medizinische Hochschule Hannover (MHH) is the reference hospital, reachable on +49 511 532 0. Drivers carry this alongside contact details for Continental-Plaza, the Hannover Messe grounds, and the hotel of record, given how far apart these locations can sit within a single day's schedule.
