Seoul presents a low risk profile for visiting executives. The National Police Agency Crime Statistics 2024 record low violent crime citywide, with Gangnam-gu, Yeouido, and Jongno-gu forming a well-policed corporate footprint covering Samsung and Hyundai headquarters, the National Assembly, and the principal embassy district. The FCDO advises normal precautions for South Korea (2026), and the US State Department rates the country at Level 1. The primary background factor is North Korea’s geopolitical posture, which the FCDO notes does not restrict travel but is a standard element of contingency planning for executive principals.

The corporate footprint is anchored in Gangnam, where the COEX convention centre, major international hotel brands, and multinational headquarters are concentrated. Itaewon provides additional international hospitality options and is suitable for executive accommodation; principals should note that late-night movements in Itaewon on Friday and Saturday evenings carry elevated ambient risk per NPA 2024 data. Traffic density in Gangnam and Jongno during morning and evening peak hours is a consistent operational variable, and vetted drivers familiar with Seoul’s routing are essential for predictable transfer times from Incheon International Airport (ICN) and Gimpo Airport (GMP).

Our executive protection services in Seoul cover Gangnam, COEX, and the Jongno embassy district. Vetted security drivers in Seoul provide airport transfers from ICN and GMP and intercity cover to Busan. For regional context, see our briefings for Tokyo and Singapore.

Compliance

Security Regulations

Key regulatory requirements for operating security services in Seoul.

Firearms Policy

Korean law severely restricts civilian firearms; private security in South Korea operates unarmed under the Guard Services Act.

Licensing

Guard Services Act (Gyeongbieop, NPA registration), National Police Agency of Korea; security companies must be registered with the NPA and individual guards require formal training certification before deployment.

Foreign Operators

Foreign-national CPOs may accompany principals as part of a bespoke detail but cannot conduct commercial security work in South Korea without NPA registration through a Korean-registered security company.

Local Intel

Zone Intelligence

Lower-Risk Areas

  • Gangnam-gu (Samsung, POSCO headquarters, luxury hotels): lower NPA 2024 crime rates across the central business district.
  • Yeouido (financial island district, National Assembly): well-policed and consistent with lower NPA 2024 crime rates.
  • Itaewon (international hospitality): lower crime outside late-night entertainment hours; preferred for HNWI hotel accommodation.
  • Jongno-gu (government, embassy, historic district): consistent police presence and active CCTV coverage.

Elevated-Risk Areas

  • Itaewon after midnight on weekends: NPA 2024 records elevated alcohol-related disorder on weekend late nights.
  • Dongdaemun market periphery at night: reduced street lighting and lower police density after 23:00.
