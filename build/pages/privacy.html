Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

[Brand] operates a security operator matching service. We connect clients who need personal security, executive protection, and related services with vetted, locally licensed operators in high-risk cities. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

What we collect

We collect only the information needed to respond to your enquiry and match you with a suitable operator:

  • Enquiry form data: Name, email address, phone number (if provided), destination city, service type, travel dates, urgency level, and a description of your requirements.
  • Website analytics: Anonymous page visits, referral source, device type, and browser. We do not use analytics that track you across websites.
  • Communication records: Emails, Signal messages, or other correspondence between you and our team during the enquiry and booking process.

We do not collect payment card details on our website. Payments are handled by separate invoicing.

How we use your data

Your data is used for three purposes:

  1. Responding to your enquiry. We review your requirements and match them against operators in our network. This may include sharing relevant details (your destination, dates, and service needs) with a specific operator to confirm availability.
  2. Service coordination. If you proceed with a booking, we share the information your operator needs for the engagement: your name, contact details, itinerary, and any security-relevant briefing notes you provide.
  3. Website improvement. Anonymous analytics help us understand which pages are useful and which cities see the most demand.

Who we share data with

We share your personal data only in these situations:

  • Matched operators. When we identify a suitable operator for your request, we share the information they need to assess and deliver the engagement. This happens only after you approve the match.
  • Legal obligations. If we are compelled by law enforcement or court order to disclose data, we will comply with the applicable jurisdiction's requirements.

We do not sell personal data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not run retargeting campaigns based on enquiry submissions.

Data retention

Enquiry data is retained for up to 12 months after your last interaction with us. This allows us to handle follow-up requests without asking you to resubmit information. After 12 months of no activity, your data is deleted.

Anonymous analytics data (no personal identifiers) is retained indefinitely for trend analysis.

If you ask us to delete your data earlier, we will do so within 30 days. See "Your rights" below.

Data security

All form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Our website is hosted on infrastructure with standard security controls including DDoS protection, access controls, and regular security updates.

For clients requiring additional confidentiality, we offer communication via Signal and PGP-encrypted email. These channels are available on request.

No system is completely secure. We apply reasonable measures proportionate to the sensitivity of the data we handle.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Session management if you interact with forms. These are first-party and expire when you close your browser.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage tracking to understand site performance. No personal identification. You can block these in your browser settings without affecting site functionality.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party social media widgets.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your data. We will do so within 30 days unless we have a legal obligation to retain it.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data. Given that we process data only for the purpose of responding to your direct request, this is unlikely to apply, but we will honour it.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@[domain].

International transfers

Our network operates across 15 countries. When we match you with an operator in your destination city, your enquiry data is shared with an operator who may be based in a different jurisdiction from you. By submitting an enquiry for security services in a specific city, you acknowledge that relevant data will be shared with operators in that city.

Children

Our services are not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version. If changes are material (for example, new data sharing partners or changed retention periods), we will note the change prominently on this page.
