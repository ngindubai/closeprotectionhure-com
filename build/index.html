Vetting

What "vetted" actually means

Three tiers. Every operator passes Bronze before they appear in our system. Only Silver and Gold operators are matched to clients.

Bronze: listed

Identity and compliance

  • Business registration verified
  • Security licence confirmed against issuing authority
  • Professional liability insurance current
  • Director identity verified (government ID)
Silver: verified

Background and competence

  • Criminal background check (DBS, FBI equivalent, or local)
  • 3+ professional references contacted and verified
  • CP/EP training qualifications confirmed
  • First aid certification current
  • Video interview passed
Gold: approved

Operational excellence

  • Everything in Silver, plus:
  • Operational assessment completed on-ground
  • 3+ client engagements reviewed
  • Client feedback above minimum threshold
  • Featured operator status
